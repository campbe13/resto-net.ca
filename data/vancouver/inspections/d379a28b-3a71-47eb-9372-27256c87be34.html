Improper Cleaning, Sanitizing of Equipment and Utensils

There was no sanitizer detected in the final rinse of the dishwasher. All dishes/utensils are to be washed manually. The three step process is: >wash with warm water and detergent >rinse with warm water >sanitize using a bleach solution (1 ounce of unscented bleach per gallon of water) This method was reviewed with the operator. The wash and rinse steps can be done in the dishwasher. The sanitizing step must be performed in a clean sink. Monitoring of the sanitizer level should be performed daily using test strips.

Please adjust/repair so that the final rinse is achieving a minimum of 50 ppm of chlorine at the dish level.
