In Compliance - Food

High temperature dishwasher was operating at 78.3 C, at the final rinse cycle. Quaternary ammonium sanitizer was tested at 200 ppm, ensure the spray bottles are used throughout the day for appropriate sanitizing.

Food handling practices appear to be satisfactory.

Refrigeration temperatures are satisfactory (< 4 degrees C / 40 degrees F).

Freezer temperatures are satisfactory (< -18 degrees C / 0 degrees F).

Hot holding temperatures are satisfactory (> 60 degrees C / 140 degrees F).

Mechanical dishwashing procedure is satisfactory (final rinse water temperature is > 71 degrees C / 160 degrees F for 10 seconds, measured at the dish; or >82 degrees C/179 degrees F, measured at the manifold).

Lack of/Inadequate Food Safety Plan

Ensure internal thermometers are placed into all coolers.

 1 0

Inadequate Cooking and/or Reheating of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

Paneer was stored on the hot table. It was tested and recorded at 48 C. This product is a potentially hazardous food product. It must be stored at 60 C and greater. Do not store foods in the Danger Zone, which is from 4-60 C. Place the product at this temperature or store in the cooler at 4 C and below.

 1 0

Food Not Protected - General

Do not store food products directly on the floor. Observed food product in the walk-in cooler, in this fashion.

 1 0

Improper Sanitary Facilities

Handsink must be readily available. At the time of the inspection, the sink was covered and not used for handwashing purposes.

 1 0

Improper Construction/Maintenance/Sanitation of Establishment

Organize and declutter the dry storage shelving units.

 1 0

Other - Misc

Mango lassi is produced every 24-48 hours. This product is not pastuerized and contains yogurt and mango pulp. Provide the food safety plan on this product. Label and dating the product, and creating a log as to how much and when the product is produced, creates a healthier environment.
