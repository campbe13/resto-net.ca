Lack of/Inadequate Food Safety Plan

OBSERVED: A food safety plan is not available for the premise.

Please ensure that a food safety plan is created for the premise. An example has been set to you. Please submit the plan for review by the above date.

 1 0

Inadequate Cooling and/or Refrigerated Storage of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

OBSERVED: The prep cooler with a temperature of 10C. This was verified by the laser thermometer and the thermometer inside of the cooler

Ensure that all refrigeration unit are at 4C or less to prevent bacterial growth. Check the thermometer that is inside the cooling unit-it is there for that purpose. All hazardous food items (deli meats, cheese, tomato sauces) have been moved to the refrigerator beside the unit which was at 4C. The owner contacted someone to adjust the temperature. Do not store food items into that refrigerator until it can hold a temperature of 4C or less

 1 0

Improper Cleaning, Sanitizing of Equipment and Utensils

OBSERVED: Sanitizer was not available for surface sanitizing. Wips cloths were used to wipe with bleach however they are stored at room temperature.

Ensure that wiping cloths are in contact with bleach at all times by remaining in a 200ppm concentration of bleach (1tsp to 1 litre of water). This will prevent bacterial growth. Ensure that a daily concentration is made and changed regularly when dirty.

 1 0

Food Not Protected - General

Garbage bags are not food grade material and can potentially leach harmful chemicals into food items. Replace all of the garbage bags with food grade plastic bags.

OBSERVED: Garbage bags used to store fresh pizza dough in the prep cooler
