In Compliance - Food

Coolers maintain 4 degrees C or less. Freezers maintain -18 degrees C or less. Hot holding units maintain 60 degrees C or hotter. Temperature monitoring program is in place. Handwashing stations are provided with liquid soap and paper towels, hot and cold running water. General sanitation of premises is satisfactory. Food Safety training requirement is met at this time. High temperature dishwasher reaches 76 degrees C at plate level during final sanitizing rinse. Bar area glasswasher dispenses 50 ppm chlorine during final sanitizing rinse. Chlorine test papers are available for glasswasher/sanitizing solution checks. Sanititzing solution is available. Pest control program is in place. No obvious signs of pests were observed at this time. Washrooms are generally well supplied. Richmond Public Health Protection bylaw signs are in place.
