Food Not Protected From Contamination - Microbiological

Meat Marinade At the time of the follow-up inspection meat was being marinaded in a large container on the floor. Food must not be prepared or stored on the floor at any time. Meat Thawing At the time of the follow-up inspection, two large pieces of meat were being thawed uncontained on a food contact surface at room temperature. This is poor practice, meat must only be thawed within a fridge or under cold running water.

 2 0

Food Not Protected From Contamination - Microbiological

Rice THE FOLLOWING VIOLATION HAS NOW BEEN RESOLVED - Rice is being left, uncovered, at room temperature after cooking and was measured at +12C. Foods must be covered after preparing and refrigerated within two hours after cooking. Rice - hydrating THE FOLLOWING VIOLATION HAS NOW BEEN RESOLVED - Rice, whilst being hydrated, is being stored at room temperature in containers located on the floor beneath the dishwasher and on top of the grease trap. Prepared foods must be covered and refrigerated. It is not acceptable to store food on the floor or on the greast trap an any time.

 2 2

Food Not Protected From Contamination - Other Sources

Hair THE FOLLOWING VIOLATION HAS NOW BEEN RESOLVED - Staff are preparing food with long hair unprotected. Hair should be tied up or covered during food preparation/cooking. - staff hair now protected.

 1 1

Improper Cleaning, Sanitizing of Equipment and Utensils

Dishwasher THE FOLLOWING VIOLATION HAS NOW BEEN RESOLVED - The supply container for the sanitizer was completely dry. Cleaning and sanitizing supply chemicals must be monitored daily and sanitizer concentration within the final rinse water should be monitored. Monitoring test strips are available from large food suppliers. Degreaser/Sanitizer THE FOLLOWING VIOLATION HAS NOT BEEN RESOLVED - Food contact surface cleaners are not readily available. Both degreaser for cleaning and sanitizer for disinfecting should be readily available in, for example, clearly labeled spray bottles.

 2 2

Inadequate Insect/Rodent Control

Mice THE FOLLOWING VIOLATION HAS NOT BEEN RESOLVED - Widespread evidence of mice was observed within the kitchen. All areas of the facility must now be thoroughly cleaned and sanitized and the activity brought to the attention of your pest control contractor for advice and action on eradicating mice from the facility. - evidence of mic activity was still observed, this must now be controlled.

 1 0

Improper Construction/Maintenance/Sanitation of Establishment

Light Fitting THE FOLLOWING VIOLATION HAS NOT BEEN RESOLVED - The light fitting over the grill beneath the exhaust ventilation hood is not working. This should now be repaired. Fridge Door THE FOLLOWING VIOLATION HAS NOT BEEN RESOLVED - The hinges to one of the doors to the prep fridge are broken. These should now be repaired.

 2 0

Food Handler Training

Food Safety Training THE FOLLOWING VIOLATION HAS NOT BEEN RESOLVED - At the time of inspection persons working at the facility did not have Food Safe Training. At least one person with Foodsafe Level I training must be working at the facility at all business operation times. - owner/opetator to take food safety training by Christmas 2011.

 1 0

Other - Misc

Rear Door THE FOLLOWING VIOLATION HAS NOT BEEN RESOLVED - The area in front of the rear door is cluttered with miscellaneous items. These will both serve as harbourage for pests and obstruct the emergency exit route through the premises. This area should now be cleared. Wash Hand Basin THE FOLLOWING VIOLATION HAS NOW BEEN RESOLVED - The wash hand basin within the restaurant serving area was not provided with a soap dispenser or clean dry hand towels. These must now be provided. Domestic Fridges THE FOLLOWING VIOLATION HAS NOW BEEN RESOLVED - Domestic fridges are provided in the serving area. These contain dairy and other perishable foods. Domestic fridges must only be used for storing sealed cans/bottles of drink etc. - fridges only used for non-perishable food items.
