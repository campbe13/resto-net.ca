Inadequate Cooling and/or Refrigerated Storage of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

Shrimp being stored in the refrigerated drawers under the grill were much too warm (13 degrees C). This is unacceptable. Immediate corrective action is required. Potentially hazardous foods must be maintained at 4 degrees C or colder at all times. Temperature monitoring must take place. Results are to be recorded in a logbook. Third Warning.

 1 0

Inadequate Hot Holding of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

Teriyaki sauce was being stored at 49 degrees C in the front grill area. This is not acceptable. Potentially hazardous foods must be maintained at 60 degrees C or hotter at all times. Temperature monitoring must take place. Immediate corrective action is required. Second Warning.

 1 0

Improper Cleaning, Sanitizing of Equipment and Utensils

Please ensure that all food contact surfaces are washed and sanitized according to the procedure outlined on the chart provided. (WASH-RINSE-SANITIZE-AIR DRY). Always sanitize dishes, cutlery and counters according to the dilution rate for water and bleach as outlined on the chart. Second Warning. Immediate corrective action required. Visible debris was again noted on the slicer. Please ensure that the slicer is properly cleaned and sanitized after each use as per the information provided. Second Warning. Immediate corrective action required. The rice scoop and the vegetable ladle were being stored inside a jar of water at room temperature. This is not a safe food handling practice. Scoops and ladles being used to dispense rice and vegetables must be cleaned and sanitized after each use or must be stored in a manner that precludes bacterial growth - either at 4 degrees C or colder or at 60 degrees C or hotter. Immediate corrective action is required. Second Warning.

 3 0

Improper Sanitary Facilities

Paper towels must be provided at the front hand wash sink. Immediate corrective action required.

 2 1

Inadequate Insect/Rodent Control

Mouse droppings were noted in the storage locker, as well as by the safe. All mouse droppings must be removed. Both the storage locker and the main food service area must be closely examined to check for further signs of rodent activity. Bags of rice must be stored in mouse-proof containers equipped with tight-fitting lids. A new door sweep must be installed on the storage room door to prevent the entrance of mice. Immediate corrective action is required.

 2 0

Improper Construction/Maintenance/Sanitation of Establishment

Paper towels and cardboard must be removed from the grill. The glass coolers in the storage locker again were dirty on the bottom. The coolers must be thoroughly cleaned. Unpainted shelves in the storage locker must still be painted so that they are smooth, washable and non-absorbent. All goods must be stored at least 6 inches (15 cm) up off of the floor. There must be at least 6 inches (15 cm) of clearance between the shelves and the floor. Second warning. The chest freezer lids are damaged and must be replaced. Gas cylinders must be securely anchored to prevent them from falling. All beverages in the front service area must be stored at least 6 inches (15 cm) up off of the floor. The plastic shipping wrap must be removed from the side of the ice machine, as well as from the side of the upright cooler located in the back food preparation area. The wall under the 3 compartment sink requires thorough cleaning.

Food Handler Training

At least one person must be present at all times who has completed Foodsafe or an equivalent food handler training program.
