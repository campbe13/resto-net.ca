Inadequate Cooling and/or Refrigerated Storage of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

Noodle rice products stored without refrigeration at the front display area.

It is unacceptable to store potentially hazardous items at room temperature. If you are not sure if product is shelf stable then testing is required by a laboratory with proof that it is a non hazardous food and can be stored safely at room temperature. Note : Consider this a second warning - further violation may involve the issuance of a fine.

1. Exhaust filters in poor sanitary condition. 2. Touch points around door handles not being cleaned regularly. 3. Crumbes and old food scraps under equipment.
