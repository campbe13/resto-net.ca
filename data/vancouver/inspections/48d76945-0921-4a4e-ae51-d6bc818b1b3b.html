Improper Cleaning, Sanitizing of Equipment and Utensils

The bleach sanitizing solution was too strong. Please note that to make up a sanitizing solution you should be using 1 teaspoon of unscented bleach per 1 litre of water to end up with a 200 ppm chlorine solution. This was remade during the inspection. Please obtain chlorine test strips so that you can check that you are using the correct amount of chlorine sanitizer in your spray bottles as well as for your manual dishwashing. The strips are available at the following retailers: Russell Foods -1255 Venables Street, Vancouver, BC V6A 3X6, (604) 253-6611; www.russellfood.ca/catalogue.htm Sysco Vancouver - 1346 Kingsway, Port Coquitlam, BC, (604) 944-4410; info@van.sysco.ca GFS (Gordon Food Service) - 1700 Cliveden Ave, Delta, BC V3M 6T2, (604) 540-5400; Planet Clean - 1609 Derwent Way, Delta, BC (604) 540-5312; Clear Tech Industries - (604) 272-400; orders@cleartech.ca Anachemia - 10-3571 Viking Way, Richmond, BC (604) 270-2252 Dunlevy Food Equipment - 60 W 7th Ave, Van, BC (604) 873-2236

Food Handler Training

At least 1 person on any shift at a permitted food facility must hold the foodsafe level 1 certificate. Please complete the course.
