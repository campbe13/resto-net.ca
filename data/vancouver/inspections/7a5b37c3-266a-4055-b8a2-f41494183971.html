Food Not Protected From Contamination - Microbiological

Operator momentarily placed plastic wrapped raw meat on cutting board being used to chop vegetables.

Raw meats should never be placed directly onto counter tops; they should be placed into a container which can then be put into the dishwasher after use for cleaning and sanitizing.. Need to sanitize cutting board.

Inadequate Cooling and/or Refrigerated Storage of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

This is the second inspection in a row which has noted potentially hazardous foods being left out on counter. Need to ensure the tofu portions are ALWAYS in the refrigerator. Also alphafa spouts are a food item which must always remain in the refrigerator.

Tofu portions for miso soup left on counter in water. Temperature of items 22c. Product discarded.

Inadequate Cooling and/or Refrigerated Storage of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

Large 3 door cooler operating at 7c. Thermometer present also indicating higher than permitted temperatures. One of the doors of the cooler appears to have difficulty closing correctly.

Coolers need to operate below 4c / 40f at all times. Ensure cooler doors are all closing and sealing correctly. Monitor cooler temperatures and record at least twice a day. Adjust cooler thermostat as required.

Improper Cleaning, Sanitizing of Equipment and Utensils

Bleach sanitizing solution on hand but it is only used at the end of the day. Cleaning cloths are just washed and continually reused throughout the day.

Food contact surfaces and equipment need to be cleaned and sanitized AT LEAST once every two hours. Cleaning cloths always need to be stored in a sanitizing solution after being washed to kill the bacteria present and not just left on the counter. Recommend the purchase of a second food prep cutting board so when its time to clean and sanitize can switch to the other previously cleaned and sanitized board and send the dirty board to the be washed, sanitized and air dried.

 1 0

Improper Cleaning, Sanitizing of Equipment and Utensils

Scoop for rice stored in a container of water which is emptied and refilled hourly.

Emptying and refilling the container does not do anything to remove the bacteria which might be present in the container and on the rice scoop. Both the scoop and the container need to be cleanded AND sanitized when changing the water. Recommend the purchase and use of a second scoop and container so recently cleaned and sanitized items can be put into immediate use while the dirty items are sent off to be cleaned and sanitized
