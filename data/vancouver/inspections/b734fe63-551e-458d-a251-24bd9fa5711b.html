Other - Misc

Hand Towels As a condition of giving approval for business licence, it was asked that you provide hand towels at the dedicated wash hand basin, these were not provided. Hand towels were also not provided at the staff wash room.

The towel dispenser should now be moved from the two compartment sink to the wash hand basin and hand towels also provided in the staff wash room.

Cleanable Surfaces There are several shelves and a table/work bench that are constructed of wood which has not been sealed and cannot be effectively cleaned.
