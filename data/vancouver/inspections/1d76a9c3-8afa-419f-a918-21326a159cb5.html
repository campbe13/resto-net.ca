Inspection Details

Inspection Date: 28-Jul-2011
Inspector:
Follow-up Required: False
Reason for Inspection: Re-inspection
Action Taken: Verbal Directive, Written Directive
Referrals:
General Comments: Discussed inspection report with the site manager, Leah. Hand delivered food permit to the operator. Ensure this is situated in a conspicuous location. Ensure temperatures are monitored on a daily basis. Logs were provided for this purpose. The concentration of the quaternary ammonium was found to be very strong, approximately 600-700 ppm. Reduce the concentration with water and use litmus strips to determine the concentration. It must be 200 ppm minimum.
Closing Comments:

Inspection Information

Observations # of New Observations # of Resolved Observations

Inadequate Hot Holding of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

Operator has obtained a probe thermometer to monitor the internal temperature of the hot holding units. Ensure these foods are stored at a minimum of 60 C and greater. Container lids were placed on-site and replenish foods as required.

 0 0

Improper Cleaning, Sanitizing of Equipment and Utensils

Quaternary ammonium sanitizer was tested and recorded at 600 ppm.

Quaternary ammonium sanitizer must be provided at 200 ppm. Use the following steps: 1. use a minimum of 200 ppm of quaternary ammonium 2. use a container and immerse all dishcloths into this solution OR use a spray bottle 3. use during the daytime and sanitize all surfaces, utensils and equipment.

 1 0

Improper Sanitary Facilities

A papertowel dispenser was placed at the two compartment sink, which is used as a handwashing basin, as well.

 0 1

Food Handler Training

Ensure the assistant manager or other employees are also certified with Foodsafe training or its equivalent. Site manager, Leah is certified with Foodsafe.

A minimum of 1 trained food handler must be present on-site at all times food is being handled or prepared. Immediately register other food handlers so that this requirement can be met. See website at: http://www.foodsafe.ca. Fax written confirmation of registration to inspector within one week.
