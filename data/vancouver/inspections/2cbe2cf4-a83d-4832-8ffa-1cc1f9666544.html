Food Handler Illness/Hygiene/Habits

Explained and performed the proper handwashing procedures to staff at time of inspection. Ensure proper handwashing practices are maintained.

Food handlers were asked to perform their handwashing techniques. Food handlers found to be washing hands improperly.

 1 0

Inadequate Cooling and/or Refrigerated Storage of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

Ensure all coolers are maintained at 4C or colder. All potentially hazardous foods requiring cold storage to be stored at a temperature of 4C or colder. All other potentially hazardous foods stored above 4C for 2 hours or greater are to be discarded immediately.

Prep cooler found to be greater than 4C at time of inspection. Cooler temperature was measured to be around 10C to 14C at time of inspection. The beef utilized to cook in the beef noodle soup was measured to be at 1C at time of inspection as it had been brought out from the cooler. Operator was directed to cease use of the prep cooler until the cooler can maintain a temperature of 4C or colder. All potentially hazardous foods that were stored in the cooler at greater than 4C for 2 hours or greater were discarded at time of inspection.

 1 0

Inadequate Cooling and/or Refrigerated Storage of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

Bean sprouts were washed and stored at room temperature at time of inspection. The bean sprouts had been at room temperature for less than 2 hours. Operator directed to store the bean sprouts inside their reach in cooler at 4C or colder.

Ensure all coolers are maintained at 4C or colder. All potentially hazardous foods requiring cold storage to be stored at a temperature of 4C or colder. All other potentially hazardous foods stored above 4C for 2 hours or greater are to be discarded immediately.

 1 0

Improper Cleaning, Sanitizing of Equipment and Utensils

The operator of this premises is directed to keep wiping cloths clean and store them in a 100 ppm chlorine solution in the kitchen while in-use to prevent the growth of pathogens.

Dirty rages left stored on countertops in the kitchen during this inspection.

 1 0

Food Not Protected - General

Dishes stored on dirty racking in the mechanical room onsite. Food stored uncovered in the walk-in cooler and in open containers in the dry storage room. Boxes of food stored on the floor of the walk-in freezer. Bowls used to scoop dry food ingredients in the dry storage room.

Ensure all food and food contact surfaces are stored in a sanitary manner to protect them from contamination at all times. Only use foodgrade scoops with handles pointing out to handle/dispense dry food ingredients.

 5 0

Improper Sanitary Facilities

Cross connection observed. Hose attached to a faucet observed hanging in a pot onsite, creating a cross connection and risk of backflow into the drinking water supply.

Directed the operator of this premises to disconnect or shorten hoses to prevent them from hanging into fluids.

 1 0

Improper Construction/Maintenance/Sanitation of Establishment

Hard-to-reach areas of flooring and equipment require thorough cleaning at this time to remove spilled food and grease accumulations. Examples of areas requiring cleaning include underneath the deep fryer, the dry storage room, and underneath coolers and shelving units onsite. Range hoods require professional cleaning, as indicated by the service sticker on these hoods.

Have this cleaning completed by September 20, 2011.
