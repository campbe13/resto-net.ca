Smoking Within 3 (6) meters of Entrances

-Observed large amount of cigarette butts just outside of east exit door closest to main bar. Additionally, observed staff smoking in this area just prior to restaurant opening for dinner service. SMOKING IS PROHIBITED WITHIN PATIOS (CUSTOMER SERVICE AREAS) [Vancouver Health By-law #9535] 4. SMOKING IS PROHIBITED WITHIN SIX METRES OF "CUSTOMER SERVICE AREAS" [Vancouver Health By-law #9535] *Owner/operator is responsible for ensuring that staff and patrons are in compliance with the above legislation.
