Food Not Protected From Contamination - Microbiological

Bean Sprouts THE FOLLOWING VIOLATION IS NOT RESOLVED - Bean sprouts were being stored in water at room temperature. Bean sprouts must only be stored at +4C or colder. - bean sprouts again observed in room temperature water. Food Handling THE FOLLOWING VIOLATION IS NOT RESOLVED - During the inspection it was observed that staff were placing food on plates using their hands, no hand washing was observed and no tongs are in use. To prevent cross contamination of food staff should not manually handle food but use tongs or have frequent hand washing practices. I recommend that tongs are now provided and ensure separate tongs are used for handling raw meat/fish etc and other foods. Tongs should be frequently cleaned in the dish washer. - food continues to be manually handled

Improper Cleaning, Sanitizing of Equipment and Utensils

Degreaser/Sanitizer THE FOLLOWING VIOLATION IS NOW RESOLVED - Neither degreaser or sanitizer was readily available for food contact surface cleaning. Both degreaser for cleaning and sanitizer for disinfecting should be readily available in say spray bottles for example. THIS IS A REPEAT VIOLATION Wiping Cloths THE FOLLOWING VIOLATION IS NOT RESOLVED - Wiping cloths were observed in several locations within the kitchen at room temperature. Wiping cloths should be clean dedicated in use and stored in sanitizer when not in use. - wiping cloths were not observed stored in sanitizer

Inadequate Insect/Rodent Control

Mice and Cockroaches THE FOLLOWING VIOLATION IS NOT RESOLVED - There is evidence of both mice and cockroaches at the facility. The whole facility must now be thoroughly cleaned and sanitized including the non-food areas, the services of a pest control contractor sought for advice and action, holes where mice may ingress the facility searched for and sealed closed and the back door kept closed except when in use. THIS IS A REPEAT VIOLATION - a pest control company has not yet been sought for eradication of mice and cockroaches. - the back door was again open without screening and screen has not been repaired. Pest Control Screen THE FOLLOWING VIOLATION IS NOT RESOLVED - The pest control screen is now in poor repair and has several holes in it. This must now be fully repaired or replaced. The screen must be used at all times the rear door held open for reasons of ventilation. - the back door was again open without screening.

Improper Construction/Maintenance/Sanitation of Establishment

Cleaning THE FOLLOWING VIOLATION IS NOW RESOLVED - All areas of the facility are in need of cleaning. All shelves, floors and work equipment should now be thoroughly cleaned particularly beneath and behind work equipment. THIS IS A REPEAT VIOLATION. Make-up Air THE FOLLOWING VIOLATION IS NOW RESOLVED - The make up air ventilation outlets and the surrounding ceilings are dirty indicating the make-up air purification filters are in need of changing. These shouId now be replaced and it is recommended that these are replaced every three months. Exhaust Ventilation THE FOLLOWING VIOLATION IS NOT RESOLVED - The exhaust ventilation ducting is now due overdue for cleaning. For good fire precautions these should be cleaned at least once every six months. - cleaning has allegedly been organised.
