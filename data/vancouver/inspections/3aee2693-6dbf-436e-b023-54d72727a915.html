In Compliance - Food

Refrigeration temperatures are satisfactory (< 4 degrees C / 40 degrees F).

Freezer temperatures are satisfactory (< -18 degrees C / 0 degrees F).

Manual dishwashing procedure is satisfactory (3 compartment sink method in use with a final rinse chlorine concentration > 100 ppm with water > 45 degrees C/113 degrees F for 2 minutes).

Handwashing facilities are satisfactory.

Maintenance of premises is satisfactory.

Washrooms are clean and well-supplied.

Thermometers are provided in every cooling unit containing perishable food.

TF: Trans Fat documentation is kept on site and provided to the EHO on request

TF: All soft spreadable margarine & oils meet <2% trans fat content requirement

TF: All other foods meet the restriction of 5% trans fat or less of total fat

Food Not Protected - General

Dry Foods At the time of the inspection there were opened bags of dry foods. Opened bags of dry foods should be stored in food grade containers with fitted lids to prevent risk of cross contamination.

 1 0

Inadequate Insect/Rodent Control

Mice There was some evidence of mice activity at the facility noted on the front shelf beneath the serving counter and in the area of the washroom. The facility should now and routinely be thoroughly cleaned and sanitized and the services of a pest control contractor sought for eradication of mice from the facility.

 1 0

Improper Construction/Maintenance/Sanitation of Establishment

Cleaning Generally cleaning needs improving in the more difficult to reach areas particularly but not limited to: shelves, cupboards, beneath work equipment and the mobile pizza dough proving shelves. A routine cleaning program should be set up for cleaning all areas of the facility regularly. THIS IS A REPEAT VIOLATION Pizza Proving Shelf Units These two units were dirty with oil, sesame seeds and flour. Plastic sheets hang over the units to retain the heat, these sheets are not replaced regularly and are impossible to clean efficiently. These units are not intended for use as proving units but more for storing pizzas within refrigerated units. These units should now be replaced with purpose made pizza dough proving units. Improper Construction/Maintenance/Sanitation of Establishment

Cleaning of Work Surfaces At the time of the inspection the restaurant was not busy but the food contact surfaces were in need of cleaning. No wiping cloths are provided for cleaning but paper towels are used instead. Both degreaser and sanitizer must be provided in clearly labeled spray bottles for example for cleaning and sanitizing respectively. Food contact surfaces should be cleaned/sanitized after each period of use. Grease Filters The grease filters above the deep fat fryer were in need of cleaning. These should be cleaned at least once weekly (recommended) to prevent risk of poor air quality. THIS IS A REPEAT VIOLATION Exhaust Ventilation Ducting The ducting for the exhaust ventilation system should be cleaned at least once every six months for reasons of fire precaution. This is now due for cleaning.

 2 0

Other - Misc

Time/Temperature Control At the time of the inspection pizzas on display were at room temperature and there was no record keeping of time food on display. Food must be in hot hold at +60C or hotter or on display for no longer than 2 hours after which time it should be discarded. If you wish to adopt this latter practice then you should provide a written food safety plan to state your operating practices for safe food.
