In Compliance - Food

Foodsafe or equivalent training requirement has been satisfied.

Food handling practices appear to be satisfactory.

Refrigeration temperatures are satisfactory (< 4 degrees C / 40 degrees F).

Freezer temperatures are satisfactory (< -18 degrees C / 0 degrees F).

Manual dishwashing procedure is satisfactory (3 compartment sink method in use with a final rinse chlorine concentration > 100 ppm with water > 45 degrees C/113 degrees F for 2 minutes).

No pests observed at time of inspection.

Sanitation of premises is satisfactory.

Washrooms are clean and well-supplied.

Equipment, utensils and food contact surfaces used on the premises are washed and sanitized in a manner that removes contamination.

Garbage disposal area is well-kept.

TF: Trans Fat documentation is kept on site and provided to the EHO on request

TF: All soft spreadable margarine & oils meet <2% trans fat content requirement

Lack of/Inadequate Food Safety Plan

> Ensure that the temperature of each cold-holding unit, and hot-holding unit is recorded twice a day on the log sheet. > Keep logs sheets on-site.

Temperature logs were not up-to-date at the time of inspection. The manager had taken the month of log sheet for the month of October home. No sheets were available for the month of November. Instructed the manager to keep all temperature logs on-site for verification purposes.

 1 0

Food Not Protected From Contamination - Microbiological

> Do not store raw meats above ready-to-eat items > Relocate all raw meats to the lower shelves OR > dedicate one freezer for the storage of raw meats.

Observed frozen raw hot dogs stored above hot dog buns in the reach-in freezer. Food must be reorganized, where raw meat must be stored below and away from ready to eat foods. Designated one cooler for this purpose and the other cooler for cooked/ready-to-eat food storage.

 1 0

Food Not Protected From Contamination - Other Sources

Observe plastic bags of cabbage stored beside/below the handwashing station. Instructed the manager to relocate the cabbage to a shelving unit above the 2-compartment sink. Explained that the plastic bags are not good protection for the cabbage as the bags can be stored on the ground. Place the cabbage into plastic containers and place on the shelving unit.

> Do not store food in close proximity to the handwashing station.

 1 0

Inadequate Cooling and/or Refrigerated Storage of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

Observed two opened containers of mayonnaise sitting on the shelf at room temperature. Explained to the manager that once the container is opened it must be stored in the cooler at 4 C or colder. Discarded both containers of mayonnaise at the time of inspection.

> Do not store open containers of mayonnaise at room temperature. > Read the label of all food products. Note if any product states "keep refrigerated after opening" and ensure that this is carried out.

 1 0

Inadequate Hot Holding of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

Measured the hot-holding unit to be at 34 C at the time of inspection. Instructed the manager to increase the temperature of the unit. Remeasured the unit to be at 60 C.

> Ensure that the hot-holding unit is operating at 60 C or higher at all times.

 1 0

Improper Sanitary Facilities

No soap was available in the Men's washroom at the time of inspection.

> Provide liquid hand soap in a hand pump dispenser in the Men's washroom.

 1 0

Improper Sanitary Facilities

> Do not place anything in the handwashing basin. > Ensure that it is available and accessible for use at all times.

Observed an umbrella hanging on the rim of the handwashing basin. A dedicated handwashing station is required for employees to properly wash their hands when contaminated. The handwashing station must be clear and accessible at all times.
