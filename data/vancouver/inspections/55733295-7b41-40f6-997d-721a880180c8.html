Poor Sanitary Design, Construction, Installation of Equipment and Utensils

Ice scoop being stored in the ice machine.

Ice is considered a food item and must be handled with the same level of sanitation as a customer's food order. Ice scoop needs to be stored separate from the ice in the ice bin in/on a food grade container/tray capable of being routinely washed and sanitized along with the ice scoop. Never store the scoop in the ice bin as the handle can get buried potentially contaminating the ice at that time or when a staff person reaches in to find the handle.

 1 0
