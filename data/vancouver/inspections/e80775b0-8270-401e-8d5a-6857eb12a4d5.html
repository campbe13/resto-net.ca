Food Not Protected From Contamination - Microbiological

Ice machines must be maintained in sanitary conditions. Thoroughly clean out the ice machine and ensure that it is maintained in a clean and sanitary manner. Store the ice scoop outside of the ice in a sanitary manner to prevent cross contamination of the ice when reaching for the scoop in the ice machine with potentially dirty hands.

Observed a build-up of sticky syrup and food in the ice machine by the bubble tea area. As well, the scoop was stored inside of the ice

Inadequate Cooling and/or Refrigerated Storage of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

Observed boiled seasoned whole eggs under the food preparation counter at 21C. The head chef informed that they were cooling.

Food items should be cooled quickly either by placing it in a shallow pan with ice or placing it directly into the refrigerator if not too hot. The eggs were placed in the refrigerator during the inspection. Ensure proper cooling techniques are used.

Inadequate Cooling and/or Refrigerated Storage of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

Observed meat thawing at room temperature

Ensure proper thawing practices are used such as: thawing in the refrigerator or submerging the meat in water and running cold clean water over it. The meat was place into the walk-in refrigerator during the inspection

Improper Cleaning, Sanitizing of Equipment and Utensils

A working sanitizer concentration was made with the chef during the inspection. As a reminder, all wiping clothes must be stored in the sanitizer when not in use and surfaces must be wiped using the sanitizer to ensure bacteria are destroyed. To make this sanitizer, add 1tsp of bleach to 1 litre of water. Ensure the sanitizer is changed daily and as the water gets dirty.

Observed no working sanitier concentration in the premise on the bubble tea and the food preparation side for sanitizing food contact surfaces

Improper Sanitary Facilities

General maintenance and cleaning of the bubble tea area is lacking. Sticky syrup, dirt and debris were observed through the area
