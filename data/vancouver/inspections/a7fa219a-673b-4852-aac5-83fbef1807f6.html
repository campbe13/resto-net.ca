Lack of/Inadequate Food Safety Plan

Please review your food safety plan to ensue that it is still reflective of your current menu offerings and is still representative of your current food preparation practices.

 1 0

Food Not Protected From Contamination - Microbiological

Bechamel sauce was being stored at room temperature. This is a high hazard food item that requires strict time controls. Bechamel sauce may not be stored at room temperature until a written food safety plan for this items has been developed. The food safety plan must clearly outline how the safety of the product will be assured with the use of time controls while it is being stored at ambient temperatures. After 2 hours has expired, any remaining product must be discarded. A large amount of blanched french fries was being stored at room temperature. This is not an acceptable food handling practice. Pre-cooking destroys a vegetable's natural defenses and renders it perishable. Once potatoes (or any vegetable) have been pre-cooked, they become potentially hazardous and must be kept refrigerated. Immediate corrective action is required.

 2 0

Inadequate Cooling and/or Refrigerated Storage of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

Foods being stored in the condiment wells of the hot side cooler were too warm (peas: 7.2 degrees C, linguini: 8.2 degrees C, red pepper sauce: 7.6 degrees C). Immediate corrective action is required. Potentially hazardous foods must be maintained at 4 degrees C or colder at all times. Refrigeration temperatures are currently being monitored twice a day. This is not sufficient. It is imperative that food temperatures be monitored throughout the day, especially during or immediately after busy lunch and dinner periods to determine the effectiveness of your refrigeration systems during periods of peak demand. Use a probe thermometers to monitor the temperature of foods being stored in condiment wells. Results are to be recorded in a logbook.

 2 0

Inadequate Cooling and/or Refrigerated Storage of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

Remember that it is imperative to re-heat and cool foods as quickly as possible. Hasten cooling by transferring foods to shallow containers with a large surface area and by stirring frequently to release heat energy. Cooling can also be facilitated by using ice baths or ice wands. Only cover foods once they have cooled sufficiently to be placed in a refrigeration unit.

 1 0

Improper Cleaning, Sanitizing of Equipment and Utensils

Utensils were being stored in containers of water on the cooking line. This is not an acceptable food handling practice. Utensils must be cleaned and sanitized after each use or must be stored in a manner that precludes bacterial growth - either at 4 degrees C or colder or 60 degrees C or hotter. Corrective action was taken at the time of inspection.

 1 0

Food Not Protected - General

The inside of the ice machine must be thoroughly cleaned and sanitized to remove mildew and filth. Ice must be protected from contamination at all times. Cleaning of the ice machine must be part of your regular sanitation and maintenance program.

 1 0

Improper Sanitary Facilities

The hand wash sink in the front preparation area is currently very difficult is access. The hand wash sink must be made more accessible. Immediate corrective action is required. Hot water must be restored at the janitorial sink. Immediate corrective action is required.

 2 0

Improper Construction/Maintenance/Sanitation of Establishment

The paper and tape must be removed from cooler #7. The ceiling directly in front of the meat cooler is badly damaged and must be re-finished so that it is smooth, washable and non-absorbent. The ceiling and light fixtures in the dishwashing area must be thoroughly cleaned. The peeling paint on the meat cooler frame must be removed. Thorough cleaning is required under all line coolers and inside the deep fryer cabinets. Deteriorated wall surfaces in the janitorial area must be repaired and re-painted (or re-finished). Broken wall tiles in the dishwashing area must be replaced. The shipping wrap must be removed from the side of the ice machine.
