Follow-up Required: False
Reason for Inspection: Re-inspection

General Comments: Chef present at time of inspection. Vancouver Coastal Health operates a progressive enforcement policy. Continued or repeated contraventions of the BC Food Premises Regulation is subject to service of violation ticket(s) and/or closure order.
Closing Comments: A warning letter will now be issued for failure to have adequate pest control and for poor general sanitation.

Food Not Protected From Contamination - Microbiological

Utensils THE FOLLOWING VIOLATION HAS NOT BEEN RESOLVED - Used utensils were being stored in water at +47C. Tongs etc must be stored at +60C and hotter or at +4C and colder. - utensils still stored in warm water

Inadequate Cooling and/or Refrigerated Storage of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

Prep Cooler THE FOLLOWING VIOLATION HAS NOW BEEN RESOLVED - Foods stored in containers within the top part of the prep coolers were measured at between +17 and +19C, raw egg was asked to be discarded. Food containers did not have lids or a cover provided. Food should be at +4C or colder at all times and should be monitored at least once daily. This should be investigated and steps now taken to resolve it.

 1 1

Food Not Protected - General

Thawing At time of revisit food was observed being thawed at room temperature. Food must only be thawed beneath cold running water or within a cooler at +4C or colder.

 1 0

Food Not Protected - General

Chocolate THE FOLLOWING VIOLATION HAS NOW BEEN RESOLVED - Chocolate was stored on shelves protected only by surround wrap. Opened bags of dry food should be stored in food grade containers with fitted lids.

 1 1

Inadequate Insect/Rodent Control

Ants THE FOLLOWING VIOLATION HAS NOW BEEN RESOLVED - In the area of the dishwasher ants were observed on the floor. This should now be investigated and controlled. Fruit Flies THE FOLLOWING VIOLATION HAS NOW BEEN RESOLVED - Fruit flies were observed in the area of the ice sink. The soft drinks nozzle appeared to not have been cleaned in recent time and was now partially congested with syrup. This should be cleaned after each period of use.

 2 2

Inadequate Insect/Rodent Control

Mice THE FOLLOWING VIOLATION HAS NOT BEEN RESOLVED - Evidence of mice was observed in the kitchen, rear storage areas and beneath the bench seating within the restaurant where bottles of wine are being stored. The whole facility should now and routinely be thoroughly cleaned and sanitized. Holes where mice may ingress or harbour should be identified and sealed (do not use expanding foam for this task) and the services of a pest control company should be sought to eradicate mice from the facility. THIS IS A REPEAT VIOLATION - general poor sanitation practices continue and mice droppings again observed in wine store beneath bench seating within restaurant. Water Pool At the time of the revisit, dirty water was observed pooling on top of the grease trap. This will encourage pests and should now be investigated and prevented.

 2 0

Improper Construction/Maintenance/Sanitation of Establishment

Fridge Leaking At time of revisit prep fridges were again noted leaking. This must now be investigated and repaired. THIS IS A REPEAT VIOLATION.

 1 0

Improper Construction/Maintenance/Sanitation of Establishment

Cleaning THE FOLLOWING VIOLATION HAS NOT BEEN RESOLVED - Additional cleaning is required in all of the more difficult to reach areas of the restaurant, kitchen, bar and rear storage areas. Ice Sink THE FOLLOWING VIOLATION HAS NOW BEEN RESOLVED - The ice sink was in need of cleaning. At the end of each working period the ice sink, including the underside of the heat exchanger block for the soft drinks, should be cleaned with hot water and detergent and then sanitized. Bar Sink Faucet THE FOLLOWING VIOLATION HAS NOW BEEN RESOLVED - The faucet was loose and should now be secured.

 3 2

Other - Misc

Hand Wash Station The hand wash station within the preparation area of the kitchen was not provided with hot water at the time of the inspection. The facility had been operating for several hours and the hot water to this sink had been turned off. Staff must have access to a dedicated hand wash station in this area at all times, if the faucet requires repairing/replacing this should now be carried out. Thermometers Clear and accurate thermometers should be provided in each and every refrigerator and monitored at least once daily.
