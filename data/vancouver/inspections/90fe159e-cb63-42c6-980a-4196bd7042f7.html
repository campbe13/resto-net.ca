Improper Cleaning, Sanitizing of Equipment and Utensils

There is no sanitizer available. A bleach water solution was made at this time. It was too strong. Ensure that only a 100ppm bleach solution is used on food contact surfaces. The solution was diluted at this time.

A sanitizer has been made and is at 200ppm. Test strips must be purchased to be able to verify the sanitizer concentration. Ensure that the sanitizer is changed often to keep it fresh.

A sanitation plan does not appear to be in use.

Sanitation plan has been submitted. Ensure this plan is followed as written.

Improper Sanitary Facilities

There is no designated hand sink in the kitchen. Staff wash their hands in the staff washroom. This is not acceptable as hands may be easily contaminated when leaving the bathroom. Staff must wash hands: - after using the washroom - before starting any food preparation - when visibly soiled - after handling raw meats - after touching face, hair - after smoking

A proper hand sink has been installed in the kitchen and properly stocked with dispensed soap and paper towel.
