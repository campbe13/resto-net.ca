Fa's Farm Produce & Meat Co

5751 Victoria
Vancouver BC V5P 3W5
Primary Owner: Qifa Cao
Primary Operator: Qifa Cao
Capacity: 0 Service Type: Restaurant - Take Out
Facility Web Site:

Inspection Details

Inspection Date: 22-Dec-2011
Inspector:
Follow-up Required: False
Reason for Inspection: Re-inspection
Action Taken: Verbal Directive, Written Directive, Closure
Referrals:
General Comments: Lab results revealed unsatisfactory TPC for roast pork. Procedure reviewed with operator from receiving raw product to service & handling of leftovers. A number of issues with the preparation and cooking procedure were identified. In addition, the hot holding case was not at correct temperature and sanitation was poor. Meat at various stages of preparation including fully cooked were hanging in the prep areas. As a result of today's findings a Closure Order was issued for the hot/cooked food side and the Health Permit was removed. Operator is requred to cease all cooking and food preparation. A written Food Safety Plan and Sanitation Plan must be developed & submitted and a Compliance Meeting must be held in order for consideration of having Closure Order lifted. Full cleaning of facility must also occur. Operator indicated he would not likely continue with the preparation and sale of hot food. Photos were taken and are attached to the file. Inspection conducted with Chi Man Lee who provided translation services as well.
Closing Comments:

Inspection Information

Observations # of New Observations # of Resolved Observations

Food Not Protected From Contamination - Microbiological

Bagged produce being kept on the counter by the till. Bagged raw chicken was observed resting on top, being weighed on a scale directly beside and just generally being handled in the same area. There needs to be better separation between raw foods and produce to eliminate risk of any cross contamination.

 1 0

Food Not Protected From Contamination - Microbiological

Protection of raw products from contamination from other raw products was minimal. Better care needs to be taken to ensure raw foods are not contaminating other products. Different raw meat & seafood should not be stored together- keep pork with pork, chicken with chicken, shrimp with shrimp etc.

 1 0

Food Handler Illness/Hygiene/Habits

Handwashing is the best prevention against the spread of disease and must be done properly & frequently by all staff.

Staff person cutting raw meat was observed wiping her nose with a tissue and then just rinsing hands under water. She was directed to wash her hands again using soap this time.

 1 0

Inadequate Hot Holding of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

Hot holding case was not keeping food at 60C/140F.

 1 0

Improper Cleaning, Sanitizing of Equipment and Utensils

When asked how they clean and sanitize they responded they clean & sanitize at the end of the day only. At the time of inspection a number of raw chickens were sitting on a metal counter by the sink. No cleaning or sanitizing was done to this counter once they were removed. The sink plug in was resting on this counter at one point. They were directed to clean this counter at the time of inspection however no sanitizing step was observed.

 1 0

Improper Cleaning, Sanitizing of Equipment and Utensils

All utensils- knives, skin piercer, scissors etc- must be cleaned & sanitized after each use. Then they must be stored in clean, washable storage i.e. clean containers or clean magnet strip. There did not appear to be any clean, sanitary storage for any utensils.

 1 0

Food Not Protected - General

Food found being stored on the floor inside the walk in cooler.

 1 0

Improper Construction/Maintenance/Sanitation of Establishment

Kitchen needs to be cleaned throughout. In particular: -all sinks and counters need to be cleaned & sanitized -flooring -cook line & cook equipment, including pots -knives & other utensils being used -walls -drying racks -counter used to cut vegetables -front service counters, including hot case & butcher block, scale etc -food containers/bins
