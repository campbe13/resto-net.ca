Inadequate Cooling and/or Refrigerated Storage of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

At time of inspection 3 pans of potatoes were found to be stored on top of the convection oven. 2 pans measured at >60C as they were just cooked, one pan measured at approx. 30C. The Manager stated that this pan was from the previous night and had been taken out of the cooler approx. 1 hour prior and due to the high temperature of the convection oven below, it had quickly risen to room temperature. Manager is advised that once foods are taken out of the cooler, they must immediately be cooked, or left in the cooler. Potatoes were immediately placed in the oven. Also advised the Manager that once the other batches of potatoes have been cooked, they must be hot held in an appropriate hot holding unit to ensure they maintain 60C or greater. Currently, she stated that they wait for the batch of potatoes that were made the previous day to be cooked and used up during lunch hour, before placing the other pans of potatoes in the hot holding unit.
