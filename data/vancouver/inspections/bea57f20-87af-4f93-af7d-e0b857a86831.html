Inadequate Cooling and/or Refrigerated Storage of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

FOODS OBSERVED COOLING IN POTS. INTERNAL TEMPERATURE OF 4 DEGREES CELSIUS. SMALL PORTION SIZES.

IMPROPER COOLING IS THE MOST CRITICAL IMPDROPER FOODHANDLING PRACTICE THAT CAUSES FOODBORNE ILLNESS. RAPID COOLING IS ESSENTIAL TO PREVENT THE GROWTH OF DISEASE CAUSING ORGANISIMS AND OR TOXINS. THIS IS A BASIC YET CRITICAL FOODSAFETY PRACTICE. RAPIDLY COOL FOODS USING THE METHODS BELOW: 1) COOL FOODS IN SMALL PORTIONS. 2) USE SHALLOW METAL PANS. YOU HAVE PLENTY OF THESE PANS IN YOUR FOOD PREPARATION AREA. USE THEM!!! 3) COOL FOODS USING AN ICE BATH. FILL UP A SINK WITH WATER AND ICE. IMMERSE CONTAINERS INTO BATH AND STIR FREQUENTLY.
