Inspection Information

Observations # of New Observations # of Resolved Observations

Inadequate Cooling and/or Refrigerated Storage of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

Chickpea spread and ham discarded by the operator on EHOs request. The operator must either provide a line/prep cooler for sandwich preparation or develop a time tracking system to ensure that any potentially hazardous foods sitting above 4 degree Celsius are discarded after 2 hours.

Some potentially hazardous items being stored at the room temperature on the sandwich table. No refrigeration or time control exist for these potentially hazardous items. As per information provided by the operator, chick pea spread and ham on the sandwich prep table has been left out for 3 hours.

 1 0

Improper Cleaning, Sanitizing of Equipment and Utensils

All the dishes/utensils must be sanitized in 100 ppm bleach or 200 ppm quat solution for minimum 2 minutes. The bleach dispensing pump must be calibrated to provide 100 ppm solution in the sink or the bleach be manually added and tested with the chlorine test strips each time the solution is prepared. Please note that this is a repeat/uncorrected critical violation and must be corrected immediately. Monitoring of the sanitizer levels in the 3 compartment sink is very important to ensure that this violation does not occur again. All the spray bottles must be properly lapelled.

No detectable chlorine in the 3 compartment sink. Bleach is being diluted in two steps, first in the container and then in the sink. An electronic dispensing pump is being used to add bleach into the sink from a pre diluted bleach container. The concentration and amount of bleach out of the pump being used to add bleach into the sink is unknown. No sanitizer test strips available. Sanitizer and detergent spray bottles are not properly lapelled.

 1 0

Food Not Protected - General

Proper sneeze guard or cover must be provided for food products on display. Either individually pack all the exposed products or provide some for of sneeze guard to protect it from potential contamination by the customers.

Observed baked goods sitting on the front service counter by the cashier. This is an uncorrected violation from the last inspection. Thermometer has now been provided in the small under counter fridge. Two containers with lids provided to store the flour. No open flour bags observed in the facility a the time of this inspection.

 1 2

Poor Sanitary Design, Construction, Installation of Equipment and Utensils

Expandable foam has been used to plug the holes across the kichen and in the dough holding room resulting in the rough and not easy to clean surfaces.

Remove the excessive foam and seal it with a smooth and easily cleanable material.

 1 0

Improper Sanitary Facilities

Paper towels have now been provided on all the hand washing stations in the kitchen and in the washrooms.

 0 1

Improper Construction/Maintenance/Sanitation of Establishment

The mop sink must be installed before 1st January 2012. Please note that the mop sink must also be supplied with running water to keep it clean.

Mop sink is required for the premises. As per information provided by the operator, a mop sink has already been bought and will be installed during the scheduled closing of the premises next week.
