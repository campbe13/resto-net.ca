Inspection Details

Inspection Date: 23-Nov-2011
Inspector: 188
Follow-up Required: False
Reason for Inspection: Routine
Action Taken: Verbal Directive
Referrals:
General Comments: A routine inspection was conducted and the results discussed with Cullin.
Closing Comments: Some concerns were noted with regards to the way stocks were being cooled. The cooling parameters are as follows: >From 60C to 21C in two hours and then >From 21C to 4C in 4 hours Please ensure that at a minimum these parameters are being met, if the foods can be cooled at a faster rate, that would be ideal. Due to the limited space within the kitchen, using an ice bath for may not be feasible. Perhaps cooling wands would be helpful to meet the cooling guidelines. Please develop a procedure that will meet the parameters.
