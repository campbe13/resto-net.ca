Food Not Protected From Contamination - Microbiological

Raw Chili in Oil Raw chili was being stored in oil at room temperature. To prevent risk from of growth of dangerous bacteria, raw products in oil must be stored at +4C or colder. Raw Meat Storage Raw chicken was being stored above other foods. To prevent risk of cross contamination, raw meat and other raw potentially hazardous food must only be stored below other foods. Lids Prepared foods are being stored within refrigerators without being covered. All prepared foods must have protective covers when in storage. THIS IS A REPEAT VIOLATION

 3 0

Inadequate Cooling and/or Refrigerated Storage of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

Cold Display Cold food on display was measured at +13C. There was insufficient ice below the food to maintain good temperature control. Displayed cold foods must be maintained at +4C or colder.

 1 0

Inadequate Hot Holding of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

Hot Display Hot displayed foods were measured at +43C. There was insufficient hot water to maintain good temperature control. Displayed hot food must be maintained at +60C or hotter.

 1 0

Improper Cleaning, Sanitizing of Equipment and Utensils

Sanitizer Sanitizer stations should be provided within the kitchen and within the bar area. Half teaspoon of bleach to one litre of water is required to wipe food contact surfaces and storing of wiping cloths when not in use. Spray bottles and/or buckets must now be provided and refreshed at least once daily. THIS IS A REPEAT VIOLATION.

 1 0

Food Not Protected - General

Lassi At the time of the inspection the jugs of lassi stored within the beer keg cooler were not covered. All food items in storage should be protected from contamination at all times. THIS IS A REPEAT VIOLATION

 1 0

Inadequate Insect/Rodent Control

Mice Traps Mice traps once set by the pest control contractor should remain in the same location to be effective. If disturbed during cleaning activities etc. they should be replaced in the same location. THIS IS A REPEAT VIOLATION

 1 0

Improper Construction/Maintenance/Sanitation of Establishment

Water Leak - sink There is a water leak from the waste pipe of the two compartment sink in the rear food preparation area. To prevent risk from pests, it should now be repaired. Hot Water The hot water tap within the staff washroom was leaking. To prevent risk of bulk loss of hot water the leaking tap should now be repaired. Soft Drinks Nozzle The soft drinks dispenser nozzle is in need of cleaning. This should be routinely cleaned and stored in soda water over night. Mop Sink The mop sink appears not to be used. This must be repaired, if necessary, and should be used to pour dirty mopped floor water.

 4 0

Improper Construction/Maintenance/Sanitation of Establishment

Wash Hand Sink The wash hand sink within the kitchen is not sealed to the wall and has an unsealed wooden edge. The sink unit should now be fixed to the wall and sealed. Exposed wood should be sealed leaving it smooth, impervious to water and cleanable. It's recommended that the top of this cabinet is covered in stainless seal.
