Inadequate Cooling and/or Refrigerated Storage of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

Measured the prep cooler to be at 8 to 16 C at the time of inspection. The manager was able to confirm that the coolers are working by the temperature recordings conducted at 7:30pm every day. The workers explained that they are constantly opening and closing the lid and doors to obtain food. The top prep cooler is used to store vegetables and sauces. Explained that 1/2 the amount of sauce (that contains any dairy product) should be placed in the prep cooler and the other half should be placed in the walk-in cooler. The manager agreed that this would work for them. Explained that each sauce should be labeled with a disposal time (2 hours later).

> Store half the amount of sauce in the walk-in cooler. This will prevent the whole batch of sauce from being at 10 C for more than 2 hours. > Label each container of sauce with a disposal time of 2 hours.
