Inspection Information

Observations # of New Observations # of Resolved Observations

In Compliance - Food

Refrigeration temperatures are satisfactory (< 4 degrees C / 40 degrees F).

Hot holding temperatures are satisfactory (> 60 degrees C / 140 degrees F).

Handwashing facilities are satisfactory.

No pests observed at time of inspection.

Thermometers are provided in every cooling unit containing perishable food.

 0 0

Lack of/Inadequate Food Safety Plan

No temperature logging of the cooler units is being done. Staff and management were advised that temperature logging must happen every day and at least twice a day. Temperature log sheets were provided to the manager.

 1 0

Inadequate Cooling and/or Refrigerated Storage of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

Tuna container was noted to be sitting at room temperature. Probe theremometer was used to take temperature and the temperature was found to be at 20.2 degrees Celsius. (note: under the counter cooler is not working and at time of inspection was not in use, ensure that if you plan on using this cooler unit it must be repaired and working at 4 degrees Celsius or lower).

Container of tuna was discarded, staff and management were advised of this and that the temperature must be kept at 4 degrees Celsius or lower at all times.

 1 1

Inadequate Hot Holding of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

Sausages on the hot dog rolling cart were not being maintained at 60 degrees Celsius, even after the temperature of this piece of equpiment was increased. Advised Caleb and staff to not sell this particular item. Chilli is being heated in the microwave and then kept in a plastic container in the bun warmer drawer. Temperature of the chilli was at 60 degrees Celsius but advised operator to ensure to store the chilli in glass or stainless steel food grade containers.

 1 0

Improper Cleaning, Sanitizing of Equipment and Utensils

Proper 3 compartment sink method of washing dishes is as follows: - Wash - Rinse - Sanitize; using 1/2 ounce of bleach per gallon of water. (this sanitizing solution can also be used to sanitize food contact surfaces).

Staff member did not know the proper 3 compartment sink method to washing dishes. At time of insepction no sanitizing of dishes is happening.

 1 0

Food Handler Training

Staff member working in the kiosk was not food safe certified.

Ensure that staff working directly in the kiosk are food staff certified.
