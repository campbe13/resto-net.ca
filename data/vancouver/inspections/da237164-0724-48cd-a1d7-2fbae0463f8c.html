In Compliance - Food

All refrigeration temperatures are below 4 degree Celsius. All freezer below -18 degree Celsius. Maintenace of the premises is satisfactory. Foodsafe certified food handlers available on site. The hand washing station is equipped with soap and paper towel.

Mechanical dishwashing procedure is satisfactory (final rinse water temperature is > 71 degrees C / 160 degrees F for 10 seconds, measured at the dish; or >82 degrees C/179 degrees F, measured at the manifold).

Food storage practices appear to be satisfactory.

Pest control program in place.

Thermometers are provided in every cooling unit containing perishable food.

TF: Trans Fat documentation is kept on site and provided to the EHO on request

TF: All soft spreadable margarine & oils meet <2% trans fat content requirement

TF: All other foods meet the restriction of 5% trans fat or less of total fat

Lack of/Inadequate Food Safety Plan

Daily temperature monitoring for all the refreigeration and hot holding units including the dishwasher rinse temeprature should be done. The operator is recommeded to keep a hard copy of the food safety plan on site.

Daily temperature logs are not being maintained.

 1 0

Lack of/Inadequate Sanitation Plan

Provide sanitizer test strips to test for the sanitizer concentration. Make the ready to use sanitizer solution available in the food handling areas. Keep a hard copy of the sanitation plan available in the premises. Please ensure that the instructions to make 100 ppm bleach solution are included in the plan.

Discussed with the operator the procedures to make 100 ppm bleach solution to sanitize the cutting boards and other food contact surfaces. No written instruction available on the procedures to make the correct concentration of sanitizer. No sanitizer test strips available to test for the sanitizer concentration.

 0 0

Improper Sanitary Facilities

Soap and paper towel available on the hand washing station.

 0 1

Inadequate Insect/Rodent Control

- Maintain regular contract with the pest control professional. - Clear away droppings on a daily basis so that premises can be monitored for new activity. - Sanitize affected areas with a solution of 1/2 ounce bleach per gallon water. - Ensure that building is made rodent-proof. Consult with pest control technician for mitigation strategies. - All food-contact surfaces must be sanitized at the start of each day prior to start of food preparation.

At the time of this inspection, few mouse droppings observed near the front display cooler and in the hot water tank room. This facility has a regular contract with a pest control professional.
