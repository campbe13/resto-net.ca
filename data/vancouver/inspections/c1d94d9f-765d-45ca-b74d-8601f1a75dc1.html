Improper Sanitary Facilities

1. This is a concern where if towel is not readily available then staff many be reluctant to wash hands. Dispensers must be in working condition at all times to ensure and encourage hand washing before handling food. 2. It is not acceptable to place any food or food related item in any wash room.

1. Paper towel dispensers at the front hand sink and rear handicapp wash room did not work until time spent adjusting them. 2. Soda water - food grade item placed in the rear wash room under hand sink.

Improper Construction/Maintenance/Sanitation of Establishment

1. It is not acceptable to use wood, cardboard or any other inferior absorbent, non durable, non smooth type of material as part for the processing or supporting structures for the production of food. These inferior materials will splinter, absorb dust, bacteria, grease, flour and are difficult to clean and maintain in a sanitary condition. Note they must be removed before next inspection. 2. Please be advised that cardboard is not to be (re used) at this business for covering shelving, walls, equipment, floor or for storing items or for distribution of any items. Do not store used cardboard from other business at the rear of this business. 3. Do not use absorbent materials to cover shelving. 4. Replace packaging table for a non absorbent, durable easy to clean type of table. 5. Replace scale with flaking paint and clean other scale. 6. I strongly recommend that these items are changed within the next 4 months.

1. Wooden parts are being used to support or as part of the noodle production and processing machinery. 2. Cardboard is being used to store items in and is stored outside from other business. 3. Mixing table is cover with a material that is not acceptable. 4. Rear table in packaging area is in poor condition where edges are wet and absorbent. 5. Small scale is flaking paint and other small scale is in poor sanitary condition with a build up of dough. 6. Front storage racks do not meet the required 15 cm off the floor and the wooden rack for parts is also made of poor grade material for cleaning.
