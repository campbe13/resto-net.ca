Inadequate Cooling and/or Refrigerated Storage of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

Observed 3 boxes of frozen chicken being stored at room temperature. Operator advised that they had recently been delivered.

All frozen/refrigerated foods must be stored in the freezer/cooler immediately upon arrival. Corrected at time of inspection.

1. No sanitizing solution prepared/in use at the time of the inspection. 2. Observed wiping cloths not in sanitizer solution.

Food Not Protected - General

Use scoops with handles and store the scoops so that the handles are not in contact with the food item.

Food Not Protected - General

Observed muffins being stored uncovered at the cash register where customers walk by. This subjects them to contamination.

Instructed operator to cover all muffins immediately with plastic wrap. Ensure all foods are covered and protected from contamination at all times.

Improper Sanitary Facilities

Ensure ALL handwashing stations are equipped with liquid soap and paper towels in a dispenser at all times.

Improper Construction/Maintenance/Sanitation of Establishment

Operator was instructed to discontinue use of the deep fryer in this area immediately. Deep frying must occur underneath the commercial ventilation system.

A deep fryer was found on shelving by side door without any commercial ventilation in place. Deep fryer appeared to have been used in this area. This is a potential fire hazard and can create grease laden vapours which can lead to pest attraction.

 1 0

Improper Construction/Maintenance/Sanitation of Establishment

Observed an accumulation of grease and debris in the following areas: >underneath cookline >underneath dishwasher >back storage area >hot water tank room

Clean and sanitize the entire premises thoroughly. This must be done on a DAILY basis to prevent pest attraction.

 1 0

Improper Construction/Maintenance/Sanitation of Establishment

Organization was found to be poor throughout the facility. There were items (including boxes of food) being stored in an unorganized manner on the floor throughout the back storage area and in the hot water tank room.

Declutter and organize these areas. Remove any items that are not being used on a day to day basis. ALL items must be stored on shelving at least 6 inches off the floor so that the entire floor can be seen (right into the corners). For pest control reasons, this area must be kept organized to allow for proper cleaning and to reduce potential rodent harbourage areas.
