Inadequate Cooling and/or Refrigerated Storage of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

New Observation: Potentially hazardous frozen foods thawed in an unsafe manner. Observed prawns and chicken being thawed in the sink under cold running water however, the foods were not completely immersed. Improper thawing provides an opportunity for surviving bacteria to grow to harmful numbers and/or produce toxins.

When thawing potentially hazardous foods ensure that foods are completely submerged under cold running water. Thaw chicken, meat, fish, and seafood seperately to prevent cross-contamination.

 1 0

Improper Cleaning, Sanitizing of Equipment and Utensils

Outstanding - Inadequate amount of approved sanitizer. Owner purchased a bottle of chlorine for pool water. Test strip showed 0ppm chlorine for sanitizer solution.

Directed the Owner to use regular household un-scented 5.25% bleach for preparing a sanitizer to be used for food contact surfaces and dirty wiping cloths to prevent contamination of foods and maintain the premise in sanitary conditions. To prepare a diluted bleach solution use: 1 tsp of bleach per gallon of water. Ensure all containers containing chemicals are properly labeled.

Clean and sanitize areas along side chopping blocks where knives may have left food debris.
