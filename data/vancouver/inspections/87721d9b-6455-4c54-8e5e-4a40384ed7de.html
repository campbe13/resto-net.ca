Lack of/Inadequate Food Safety Plan

OUTSTANDING ITEM: Operator to develop and submit a written food safety plan to district EHO. Choose a selection of menu items and indicate the following: > Where the food comes from (supplier) and how it is shipped (frozen, cold, etc.) > How food is stored and at what temperatures (provide daily logs of temperatures of cooler/freezer units). > How frozen foods are thawed > How food is prepared and handled (how long and at what temperature). > How it is cooked and at what temperature it is cooked to. > How it is cooled and/or stored after it is cooked (if not served right away). > How temperatures of all foods are monitored

Inadequate Cooling and/or Refrigerated Storage of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

Instructed operator to discard soup at the time of inspection as it was unclear as to how long it had been there for. Ensure that all hot foods are cooled from 60C to 20C within 2 hours and 20C to 4C within 4 hours using the following methods: 1. shallow liquid food depth -ice water bath and regular stirring -not covered until cold 2. Small solid food size -rapid chill refrigeration -not covered until cold

Observed a container of soup being cooled at room temperature with the lid on. Temperature of soup was measured to be 35C.

 1 1

Improper Cleaning, Sanitizing of Equipment and Utensils

Sanitizer (e.g. 1/2tsp bleach to 1L water) must be prepared daily and used on all food surfaces and equipment at the start of each day and throughout the day. Store solution in a spray bottle or in a bucket and ensure that it is available at all times.

No sanitizing solution prepared/in use at the time of the inspection.

Lack of/Inadequate Sanitation Plan

OUTSTANDING ITEM: Operator to develop a written sanitation checklist and post in a conspicuous location. Staff must check off each task after completion daily. Plan to include the following: >sanitizer used - how to make, how often to make, how often to use, how to test for proper concentration >how often to clean and sanitize food contact surfaces and non-food contact surfaces >dishwasher - sanitizer concentration/final rinse temperature >manual dishwashing >pest control >garbage disposal >staff hygiene - illness, handwashing, proper attire, hair restraints
