Lack of/Inadequate Food Safety Plan

>Ensure that temperatures of coolers and freezers are measured and recorded daily.

IN PROGRESS - The operator has photocopied the temperature logs and states that he will post them for the employees to fill out everyday.

 0 0

Improper Cleaning, Sanitizing of Equipment and Utensils

>Ensure food contact surfaces are regularly sanitized after being cleaned with soap and warm water. >Ensure either a spray bottle or bucket of 100 ppm chlorine bleach solution is always available. To make 100 ppm chlorine bleach solution: 1/2 tsp bleach per litre water 1 tbsp bleach per Imperial gallon water

IN PROGRESS - Observed no sanitizer made up on site, though bleach was available. Food contact surfaces must be first cleaned with soap and then sanitized. At time of inspection, a 100 ppm chlorine bleach solution was made up.

 0 0

Food Not Protected - General

IN COMPLIANCE - All food was observed to be prepared on food contact surfaces.

 0 1

Inadequate Insect/Rodent Control

>Clean and sanitize all areas where mouse droppings are observed.

IN PROGRESS - The operator has had a professional pest company come in and set up traps. Most of the mouse droppings have been cleaned up but some mouse droppings are still observed in hard to reach areas such as below shelving along the wall.

 0 0

Improper Construction/Maintenance/Sanitation of Establishment

IN COMPLIANCE - A shelving unit was placed in the back of the seating area in the restaurant. Many of the items stored on the floor had been removed. Papayas and ginger were still observed to be stored on the floor. At the time of inspection, the papayas were moved from the floor to a shelving unit.

 0 1

Improper Construction/Maintenance/Sanitation of Establishment

>Ensure the bubble tea fridge is cleaned and sanitized. >Ensure the hard to reach areas of the floors (under the shelving) are cleaned of all food debris and dirt.

IN PROGRESS - Observed grease and food debris buildup has been cleaned from equipment, shelves and some of the floor area. All cardboard has been removed. The plastic covering has been removed from the stainless steel shelves. Observed the inside of the bubble tea fridge had food debris still, and underneath all the shelving there is still food debris and dirt in hard to reach areas. A Sanitation Plan was submitted.

 0 0

Food Handler Training

IN PROGRESS - Owner provided a copy of his FoodSafe certificate.

>Fax or mail copies of FoodSafe certificates for other employees.
