Lack of/Inadequate Food Safety Plan

Monitor/record temperatures of all cooling units daily (there are 4 coolers). The cooler temperatures must be 4 degrees C at all times. Monitor temperatures of hot holding foods to ensure they are being held at adequate temperatures (hot held foods in kitchen and soup in front). Hot holding temperatures must be 60 degrees C (140F) at all times. Ensure that foods that are cooling are being cooled adequately (i.e. from 60-20 degrees C in 2 hrs or less and from 20-4 C in another 4 hrs or less). Dishwasher temperature must read a final rinse temperature of 82 degrees C leaving the manifold (on the guage). The temperature records must be available upon request from the EHO.

Temperature monitoring has now started for some coolers (however, not all coolers) and hot holding. Dishwasher temperature is not being monitored.

Food Not Protected From Contamination - Other Sources

Resolved - foods are no longer going to be stored/covered with newspaper and cardboard. Only use clean, food-grade materials (i.e. stainless steel or plastic) to store foods.

Inadequate Cooling and/or Refrigerated Storage of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

The prep cooler still has an ice build-up around the fan. The coils need replacing. Have the prep cooler serviced again as soon as possible. Store only small amounts of foods in the inserts above and replenish as needed from the cooler down below or the up-right cooler. Keep the lid closed as much as possible. Monitor and record cooler temperatures twice daily and take corrective action (i.e. servicing, relocating, discarding, etc.. if required).

Potentially hazardous food temperature in excess of 4 degrees C. Cooked potentially hazardous foods are not being cooled to 4 degree C. or less within 2 hours. 1. Resolved - up-right 2 door glass cooler operating at 4 degrees C. Thermometer inside showed 4 degrees C. 2. Undercounter prep cooler operating at 4 degrees C. Thermometer inside showed 4 degrees C. However, foods (tofu, cut vegetables, veg. burgers) in inserts still measured >4 degrees C (veg burgers and cut vegetables measured 7.8-8 degrees C).
