Handwashing is the best method to prevent the transmission of any disease. All hand wash sinks must have hot and cold running water, liquid soap and single use towels at all times. Inadequate Cooling and/or Refrigerated Storage of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

Sliced tomatoes, milk, yogurt, and cut fruits, and cooked vegetables are potentially hazardous food and must be stored below 4 degree C. Storing potentially hazardous food in room temperature promotes the growth of disease causing bacteria. All food stored above 4 degree C and below 60 degree C for longer than 2 hours must be discarded immediately. Service the ice machine. At the mean time, all potentially hazardous food stored at room temperature for longer than 2 hours MUST be discarded. After the ice machine is repaired: Store inserts on/in ice. Keep foods level low. Keep foods covered as much as possible. Measure food temperature with a metal stem thermometer and record in log. All potentially hazardous food that has been stored above 4 degree C for longer than 2 hours MUST BE DISCARDED IMMEDIATELY. POTENTIALLY HAZARDOUS FOODS (IE. MEATS, POULTRY, SEAFOOD, DAIRY PRODUCTS, COOKED PASTA/RICE/VEGETABLES, SUSHI PRODUCTS, ETC.)

Buffet salad bar is still maintained at room temperature. Manager states the ice machine is broken. A service has been arranged already. Some potentially hazardous food such as yogurt and tomatoes are replaced every two hours.

Improper Cleaning, Sanitizing of Equipment and Utensils

All utensils and food equipment must be properly cleaned and sanitized to remove disease causing bacteria to prevent the contamination of food. Service the glass washer as soon as possible. A minimum concentration of 50 ppm chlorine is required to achieve adequate sanitation. At the mean time, use the other dishwasher in the kitchen to clean and sanitize all utensils and food equipment. Test chlorine level with a test strip on a regular basis.

Mechanical glass washer has been serviced but still has zero chlorine level in the final rinse cycle at time of inspection.
