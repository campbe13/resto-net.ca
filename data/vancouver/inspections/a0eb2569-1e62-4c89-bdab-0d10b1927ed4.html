Improper Cleaning, Sanitizing of Equipment and Utensils

Keep wiping clothes and rags submerged in sanitzer when not being used. To make 100 ppm bleach sanitizer mix 1 teaspoon of household bleach with 1 liter of water. Wipe down all food preparation counters, cutting boards with sanitizer every 4 hours. Soak all cutting boards at the end of each day in 100 ppm sanitizer solution. UPDATE: ISSUES RESOLVED.

Sanitizer was not made up and being used by kitchen staff. Cutting boards are not soaked in sanitzer daily as they should be.

Improper Construction/Maintenance/Sanitation of Establishment

Following areas require cleaning and maintenance:

Remove the build up of grease and food debris underneath the deep frier. Clean food splatter on the kitchen walls, especially obove the chest freezer. Clean and sanitize the food scraps garbage can in the front Clean and sanitize inside and outside of the stand-up cooler - keep the doors closed AT ALL TIMES to maintain proper cooling temperature of 4 degrees C. Apply varnish or paint on raw wood shelves. Remove tin foil and replace the side splash next to the sinks. UPDATE: All issues have been resolved. Note the wall behind the freezer and the splash next to the sink have been freshly tiled with ceramic tile.
