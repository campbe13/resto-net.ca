Please keep the back door closed at all the times.

No signs of pest infestation in the facility. The building has a known mouse infestation problem. As per information provided by the operator a pest control contract exist for the building.

Inadequate Cooling and/or Refrigerated Storage of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

All the potentially hazardous foods must be stored below 4 degree clesius. This violation corrected at the time of this inspection. Please ensure that the meat is quickly transferred to the fridge after any preparation step.

Observed containers of raw breaded meat being stored at the room temperature.

 1 0

Improper Cleaning, Sanitizing of Equipment and Utensils

The cabbage cutter must be properly cleaned and sanitized after each use. Some parts may need disassembling in order to sanitize the hard to reach areas. The cabbage cutter cleaning and sanitizing procedures should be part of the written sanitation plan. Wiping clothes must be stored in the sanitizer solution ( 100 ppm chlorine, 200 ppm quat).

Cabbage cutter not being sanitized after each use. At the time of this inspection, one of the employee observed cleaning the cabbage cutter with a dry wiping cloth and putting it on the shelve. Visible food debris in the hard to clean areas on the machine. Presently, no written sanitizing procedures exist for the cabbage cutter. The cabbage cutter is an import from Korea and the company web site do not have any cleaning and sanitizing procedures in their manual on line. Wiping clothes not being stored in the sanitizer solution.

 2 0

Food Not Protected - General

Some packed food being stored in the staff washroom. Some clean soup bowls also observed in the shelf in the staff washroom. According to the information provided by the operator, these soup bowls are not being used.

Remove all the packed food out of the staff washroom. Remove all the unnecessary utensils out of the premises. The violation corrected at the time of this inspection.

 1 1

Improper Construction/Maintenance/Sanitation of Establishment

The line cooler must be thoroughly cleaned and sanitized and the floor of the cooler must be sealed to provide a smooth easily clean able surface. Target completion for sealing of the floor- Next scheduled inspection. All the equipment and the hard to reach corners must be thoroughly cleaned.

The floor of the line cooler is corroded and is being covered with a vinyl board. Lot of food debris observed in the corroded floor of the line cooler. Thorough cleaning is needed in all the fridges, and hard to reach corners of the facility.
