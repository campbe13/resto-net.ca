Inadequate Cooling and/or Refrigerated Storage of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

Prep Fridge The prep fridge adjacent the stove was measured at +10C. This should now be repaired to ensure a maximum temperature or +4C. All potentially hazardous and spoilable food should be removed from this fridge until it has been repaired. THIS IS A REPEAT VIOLATION Food Storage Food stored within the top containers of the prep fridge must be stored at least 1" below the lip of the container to ensure good temperature control. Food should not be stored on top of the containers and the lids of the prep coolers should be kept closed during slow business periods. THIS IS A REPEAT VIOLATION Food Stored on Ice Pasta and sauces are being stored at counter level on ice. Food was measured at +12C. For good temperature control, food should be fully immersed within the ice. Storage of food in this manner is only acceptable at busy business periods otherwise mechanical refrigeration must be used. THIS IS A REPEAT VIOLATION

Improper Cleaning, Sanitizing of Equipment and Utensils

Ice Sink The ice sink was dirty beneath the soft drinks heat exchanger plate within the ice sink. The ice sink should be cleared of ice and cleaned and sanitized after each period of use. THIS IS A REPEAT VIOLATION

Inadequate Insect/Rodent Control

Mice There is evidence of mice both within the kitchen and the bar area. All areas of the facility should now be thoroughly cleaned and sanitized, traps positioned so that the longer sides are parallel and tight to the wall with the entry holes clear and any mice holes in walls, floors and ceilings sealed (expanding foam is not suitable for this task). The rear door should now be pest proofed, there appears to be an access point in the right hand bottom corner of the door. THIS IS A REPEAT VIOLATION

 1 0

Other - Misc

Food Storage At the time of the inspection food was being stored within the common area outside the rear door. Food should not be stored in this area. Mop Sink The mop sink was being used to store miscellaneous items, it was dirty and was not being routinely used for disposal of mop water. It should now be cleared and used as intended under the terms of the permit.
