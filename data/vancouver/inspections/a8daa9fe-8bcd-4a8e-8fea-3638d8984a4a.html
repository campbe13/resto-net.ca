Inadequate Cooling and/or Refrigerated Storage of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

Food must be cooled from 60C to 20C within 2 hours and then placed in the cooler where it has an additional 4 hours to cool from 20C to 4C. To help food cool rapidly break large batches into smaller portions, spread out in shallow pans (metal is better than plastic), stir frequently, leave partially uncovered to allow hot air to escape. Ice/water baths can also be used to help food cool quickly.
