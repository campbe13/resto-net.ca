Food Not Protected From Contamination - Microbiological

Tongs THE FOLLOWING CONTRAVENTION HAS NOW BEEN RESOLVED - At the time of the inspection tongs were observed stored hanging from the hot plate. You should ensure that there is clear separation of tongs used for handling meat from tongs used for handling salad. Also staff must be made aware that under no circumstance must they handle both raw meat and salad without first washing their hands. To control bacterial growth, tongs must be stored in hot water above +60C, in ice or in sanitizer when not in use.

Improper Cleaning, Sanitizing of Equipment and Utensils

Dishwasher THE FOLLOWING CONTRAVENTION HAS NOW BEEN RESOLVED - At the time of the inspection the dishwasher filter was blocked with a large quantity of food. Food should be washed from plates using the pot wash sink before being placed within the dishwasher. It is understood that this food was left from the previous evening's trading. The dishwasher should be left clean at the end of the day's trading.

Lack of/Inadequate Sanitation Plan

Sanitation Plan THE FOLLOWING CONTRAVENTION HAS NOW BEEN RESOLVED - A written sanitation plan must now be provided and brought to the attention of staff to ensure all areas of the facility are routinely cleaned.

Mop Sink THE FOLLOWING CONTRAVENTION HAS NOW BEEN RESOLVED - The mop sink should be secured and sealed to the two walls that it adjoins.
