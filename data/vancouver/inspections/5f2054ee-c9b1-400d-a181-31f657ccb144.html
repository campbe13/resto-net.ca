Inspection Details

Inspection Date: 24-Jun-2011
Inspector: 3024
Follow-up Required: True
Reason for Inspection: Routine
Action Taken:
Referrals:
General Comments: Routine inspection with kitchen Manager Julian; General Manager Scott Gluck absent. >At 3PM several potentially hazardous foods were handled in a questionable manner. Stored at kitchen temperatures on a tray cart were trays of deep fried battered fish (lunch special), cooked chicken breasts, frozen steaks and a pan of fried smelt fish (claimed for personal consumption by staff, not for customers). The following frozen items were thawing at room temperature; chicken-halves in a pail of water (22 C) stored underneath the utility sink with bags of prawns beside it; a box of rib ends were left on the kitchen floor. >Product temperatures in the inserts at the front line coolers were marginal; particularly the cooler to the right. >The kitchen primary hand wash sink was missing a tap handle and the paper towel dispenser jams. >Use of sanitizer did not appear to be common practice. Staff was not familiar with the type of sanitizer in use or its proper dilution. >Bar glasswasher: Both supply bottles for detergent and sanitizer were completely empty. Accumulations of filth & food debris were very evident throughout the food preparation areas.
Closing Comments: Follow-up inspection Friday 24 June. Call 604-675-3839 or email should you have any questions.
