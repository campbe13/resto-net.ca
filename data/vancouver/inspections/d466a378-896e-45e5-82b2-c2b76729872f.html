Inadequate Cooling and/or Refrigerated Storage of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

Discussed this concern with the operator of this premises (chef) and a decision was made to only keep a small quantity of this product at room temperature (only an amount that would be used within 2 hours maximum) and keep the rest at 4 degrees C at all times to prevent the growth of C. Botulinum or other pathogenic organisms. Resolved immediately.

NEW CRITICAL HAZARD: -Fresh minced garlic in butter/margarine stored at room temperature at this time. Two food inserts observed at room temperature and not in immediate use.

 1 1

Food Not Protected - General

NEW ISSUES: -Improper thawing of frozen hamburgers and other potentially hazardous foods observed left at room temperature. -Cooked chicken and beef observed left at room temperature on a rear counter while not being immediately worked on/prepared.

The operator of this premises stated that thawing at room temperature is not common practice, but these foods were mistakenly not placed in the cooler to thaw last night. Directed the operator of this premises to ensure that all potentially hazardous foods are thawed in the cooler at 4 degrees C or less or under cold running water in a clean sink and to ensure that all potentially hazardous foods are stored at 4 degrees C or less or 60 degrees C at all times when not being immediately prepared.

 2 0

Improper Sanitary Facilities

NEW ISSUE: -Bar handsink not supplied at this time.

Immediately install fixed dispensers for papertowels and liquid handsoap at the bar handsink in locations not putting food materials at risk of contamination.

 1 0

Improper Construction/Maintenance/Sanitation of Establishment

NEW ISSUES: -Cleaning of hard-to-reach and hidden areas, such as along baseboards, underneath tables and equipment and underneath the grill and deep fryers, is required at this time to remove filth and grease.

 2 0

Smoking Within 3 (6) meters of Entrances

Cigarette butts observed outside within 5 feet of the rear door to the kitchen. Signage is posted on the exterior of this door.

Directed the operator of this premises to remove this smoking area and direct staff to comply with the City of Richmond's Public Health Protection Bylaw by not smoking within 6 metres (approximately 20 feet) of any door.
