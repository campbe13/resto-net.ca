Kamei Royale Restaurant

211, 1030 W Georgia
Vancouver BC V6E 2Y3
Primary Owner: Masuda Toshiaki
Primary Operator: Hitomi Ishikura
Capacity: 0 Service Type: Restaurant - Full Service
Facility Web Site:

Inspection Details

Inspection Date: 15-Nov-2011
Inspector:
Follow-up Required: False
Reason for Inspection: Routine
Action Taken: Written Directive
Referrals:
General Comments: Only pre-packaged foods may be sold in the front retail area. Dispensing of hot or cold foods is not permitted and must be discontinued immeditely.
Closing Comments:

Inspection Information

Observations # of New Observations # of Resolved Observations

Lack of/Inadequate Food Safety Plan

A written food safety plan must be forwarded to Vancouver Coastal Health for review before December 31, 2011.

 1 0

Food Not Protected From Contamination - Other Sources

A bottle marked "Quat Sanitizer" was filled with water and was being used for food preparation. This is unacceptable. Chemical bottles may never be used for food preparation. Immediate corrective action is required. -

 1 0

Inadequate Cooling and/or Refrigerated Storage of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

The front retail sushi display case was too warm (8.8 degrees C). This is unacceptable. This unit may not be used for the storage or display of potentially hazardous foods until it is capable of maintaining a temperature of 4 degrees C or colder at all times. Temperature monitoring and recording must take place throughout the day.

 2 0

Inadequate Cooling and/or Refrigerated Storage of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

Eggs were again being stored at room temperature. Two baskets of eggs were discarded by the Inspector. Eggs must be maintained at 4 degrees C or colder at all times. Pre-cooked broccoli was being stored at room temperature. This is not acceptable. Once cooked, broccoli is a potentially hazardous food and must be stored at 4 degrees C or colder at all times. BBQ eel and salmon skin were being stored on ice. This is not sufficient to keep these foods at a safe temperature of 4 degrees C or colder. BBQ eel and salmon skin must be mechanically refrigerated. Sushi rolls were being prepared well in advance of the lunch service (10:30 am). Completed rolls were being stored at room temperature. No time controls were in use. This is unacceptable. All sushi must be mechanically refrigerated until service. Immediate corrective action is required.

 7 3

Improper Cleaning, Sanitizing of Equipment and Utensils

There was no detectable sanitizer in the VIP Bar glasswasher (the sanitizer bottle had run dry). This is unacceptable. The glasswasher may not be used until it has been serviced and there is 50-100 ppm chlorine in the final rinse cycle. This concentration must be verified each time the VIP Bar is in operation. The meat slicer in the main kitchen was filthy. The slicer must be cleaned and sanitized after each use. Please refer to the guideline provided for instructions on how to properly clean and sanitize a meat slicer. The ice boxes in both the VIP Bar and the main Bar were unsanitary. In addition, the beverages lines intersecting the ice boxes were covered with mildew and the beverage nozzle holders were slimy. These findings are unacceptable. Ice intended for human consumption must be protected from contamination at all times. The ice boxes, beverage lines and beverage nozzle holders must be washed, rinsed and sanitized at the start of EACH day.

 3 0

Improper Cleaning, Sanitizing of Equipment and Utensils

Tongs and skewers in the sushi bar were being stored in a large bucket of water. This is not a safe food handling practice. Tongs and skewers must be cleaned and sanitized after use, or must be stored in a manner that precludes bacterial growth (either at 4 degrees C or colder or 60 degrees C or hotter.

 1 0

Food Not Protected - General

Raw chicken was being stored directly next to ready-to-eat foods. Also, a bowl of food was being stored directly on top of raw chicken. This is a highly hazardous food handling practice. Raw meat must be stored below or well away from other foods at all times. A food handler placed raw beef and raw pork onto the grill with his bare hands. He then proceeded to perform other food handling tasks without ever attempting to wash his hands. This is UNACCEPTABLE! Hands must be washed each time raw food is handled, before putting on and taking off gloves, or after coming into contact with any potentially contaminated surface. Please ensure that all staff are educated in the importance of hand washing. Foods were being stored directly on top of other foods without proper lids being in place. Please note that foods may only be stacked on top of each other if tight-fitting lids are in place to protect the foods from contamination. Immediate corrective action is required.

 6 3

Improper Sanitary Facilities

The liquid soap and paper towel dispensers in the men's staff washroom must be re-filled. Second Warning. The 2 compartment sink in the dishwashing area is being used for the storage of dishes and equipment. This is not the intended use of this sink. All dishware and equipment must be removed and relocated to another storage area. The hand sink at the entrance to the kitchen must be provided with paper towels. The soap dispenser must be re-secured to the wall. The hand wash sink at the far end of the sushi bar was obstructed with styrofoam. Staff reported that the sink is not is use. Please ensure that the sink is clear, accessible and operational at all times. Immediate corrective action is required.

 4 0

Inadequate Insect/Rodent Control

Mouse droppings were noted in the sushi bar (adjacent to the lobster tank). Two complaints of a rodent sighting have been received. All rodent excrement must be removed. Rodent abatement strategies must be increased. Please refer to the information provided regarding control of mice in food establishments. Rodent activity must be monitored very closely. Many of the rodent control devices in this restaurant have been rotated or moved away from the walls. Please note that moving or rotating the devices even slightly renders them ineffective. If the devices must be moved to clean the floor, please ensure that they are placed back in their exact position. Immediate corrective action is required. Immediate corrective action is also required to abate the fruit flies in the dishwashing area, as well as in the main bar (in the vicinity of the ice box). Please note that moist conditions are favorable to the proliferation cockroaches and fruit flies. Eliminate all sources of pooling water.

 2 0

Improper Construction/Maintenance/Sanitation of Establishment

The floor in the hot water tank room must be scrubbed clean. Second Warning. The areas being used to store equipment and paper goods must be cleaned and organized. Clutter is to be reduced. All items not required for the day-to-day operation of this restaurant must be removed. Second Warning. Thorough cleaning is again required in the dishwash area. There is still a bad odor in the area, indicative of unsanitary conditions and pooling water. Ensure that all equipment and walls in the dishwash area, including under the dishwash counter, are thoroughly cleaned. All deteriorated mats on the floor in the dishwash area must be replaced. The bus pans are filthy and must be thoroughly cleaned or replaced. Second Warning. Cleaning is required under all counters and coolers in the sushi bars. All wooden blocks under the cutting boards in the sushi bars must be replaced with blocks that are smooth, washable and non-absorbent. Second Warning.

 9 2

Improper Construction/Maintenance/Sanitation of Establishment

The aluminum foil behind the grill in the main sushi bar, as well as surrounding the tempura fryers in the main kitchen, must be removed. Second Warning. Thorough cleaning is required behind and beside the glasswasher in the VIP bar. Second Warning. Thorough cleaning is required inside the storage cupboards in the VIP bar. Second Warning. The storage room located adjacent to the VIP Bar is still cluttered and disorganized. Thorough cleaning of this room is required. All goods must be neatly organized. Second Warning. The counter in front of the hot water urn (in the VIP bar) is completely deteriorated and must be replaced with a new counter surface which is smooth, washable and non-absorbent. The inside ledge of the ice machine in the VIP Bar is in need of thorough cleaning. The divider next to the tempura deep fryer requires thorough cleaning.

 13 6

Improper Construction/Maintenance/Sanitation of Establishment

Black mildew behind the hand sink (at the entrance to the kitchen) must be removed. The silicon must be replaced. Thorough cleaning is required at the side and back of the grill. Second Warning. Damaged wall surfaces in the dry storage room must still be repaired. Second Warning. Walls in the dry storage room must be cleaned. The meat cooker requires thorough cleaning. The towel at the bottom of the under-counter bar fridge (next to the glasswasher) must be removed. If this refrigeration unit is leaking, it must be repaired. The plastic wrap dispenser in the kitchen requires thorough cleaning. The wooden chopping block directly next to the double burner requires through cleaning.

 15 7

Food Handler Training

At the time of the inspection, no staff trained in Foodsafe or an equivalent food handler training was available in the kitchen. This is unacceptable. At least one person per shift, including a manager, must be present in both the kitchen and the sushi bar whenever food is being prepared. Given the high number of food handling infractions noted on this report, every effort must be made to train as many food handlers as possible.
