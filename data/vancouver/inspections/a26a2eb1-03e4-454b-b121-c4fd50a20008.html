Improper Cleaning, Sanitizing of Equipment and Utensils

The sanitizing solution was not available in the kitchen. This has also been observed during the inspection on June 16, 2011 and June 30, 2011. A working sanitizer solution was located in the dim sum area which is not a convenient location tot he kitchen.

Make a 200 ppm bleach sanitizer solution by mixing 1 teaspoon of bleach with 1 liter of water in a bucket at the beginning of every shift and ensure that there is one located at each of the food preparation stations. Store wiping clothes in the sanitizer - sanitize all food contact surfaces at least every 4 hours or more often as necessary and change the sanitizer once it gets dirty Sanitizer should be applied on a clean surface i.e. after food debris is removed, and its purpose is to kill pathogens (disease-causing microbes). According to section 17 of the Food Premise Regulation: Operators of food premises must ensure that the premise and the equipment and utensils used on the premise are maintained in a sanitary manner. Failure to do so may result in a $230 fine.
