In Compliance - Food

Walk-in cooler in kitchen at 4C Prep-cooler across from grill/stove area at 4C.

Freezer temperatures are satisfactory (< -18 degrees C / 0 degrees F).

Hot holding temperatures are satisfactory (> 60 degrees C / 140 degrees F).

Mechanical dishwashing procedure is satisfactory (final rinse chlorine concentration is > 50 ppm).

No pests observed at time of inspection.

Garbage disposal area is well-kept.

 0 0

Food Not Protected From Contamination - Microbiological

A tray of mashed potato was found stored on the stainless steel table next to a chopping board with raw beef. No signs of cross contamination noted as the mashed potato had just been placed there but operator told to ensure that raw and cooked food products are not stored within the same vicinity to prevent potential cross contamination of the food products. Chef moved the mashed potato into the walk-in cooler at time of inspection.

Ensure raw meats and foods are stored separate from uncooked vegetables, and cooked food products to prevent potential cross contamination.

 1 0

Inadequate Cooling and/or Refrigerated Storage of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

Back walk-in cooler with storage of potentially hazardous foods and alcohol found to be greater than 4C at time of inspection. Temperature of the cooler was measured to be at 6C at time of inspection. Premises just had a delivery and open door to walk-in cooler may have contributed to higher temperature.

Ensure that the walk-in cooler is maintained at 4C or colder. All potentially hazardous foods requiring cold storage to be done so at 4C or colder. Potentially hazardous foods requiring cold storage found above 4C for 2 hours or greater to be discarded immediately.

 1 0

Improper Cleaning, Sanitizing of Equipment and Utensils

Bleach sanitizer concentration was greater than 200ppm concentration at time of inspection. Container found to be unlabeled and wiping cloths not stored in the sanitizer solution when not in use.

Ensure bleach sanitizer is maintained at 100ppm concentration (28ml bleach per every 4.5L water). Ensure containers are clearly labeled and stored in a readilly accessible location. Ensure wiping cloths are stored in the sanitizer solution when not in use.

 1 0

Improper Cleaning, Sanitizing of Equipment and Utensils

Ensure that the mechanical glass washing procedure is satisfactory with final rinse chlorine concentration > 100 ppm with cold water rinse >13 degrees C/55 degrees F.

Bar glass washer found to have approximately 25ppm bleach sanitizer concentration on the glass level during final rinse. Operator stated that a new glass washer is in the process of being installed within the next week. Water in the tank found to be at 200ppm concentration and sanitizer levels were high in the container. Machine had just been run and may require a few cycles before providing the required concentration at the glass level during final rinse.

 1 0

Improper Sanitary Facilities

Handwash station to be provided with both hot and cold water readily available. The attached hose is to be removed.

Kitchen area has no adequate handwashing area as single tap providing hot water is too hot to properly wash hands under the required time length. Cold water tap has a hose attached with no back flow prevention device.

 1 0

Improper Construction/Maintenance/Sanitation of Establishment

Ensure all areas of the premises maintained in a clean and sanitary state. All clutter to be removed from the premises.

General sanitation in the following areas requires correction: a) Floors of kitchen found to have food residue and requires removal and cleaning. b) Floor next to syrup containers in the back storage room found to be dirty and requires cleaning. c) Clutter (non-required) items are to be removed from the premises.
