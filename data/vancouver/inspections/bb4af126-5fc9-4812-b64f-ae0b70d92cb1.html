Inadequate Cooling and/or Refrigerated Storage of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

Ensure that all potentially hazardous food is thawed under cold running water, in a working cooler maintained at 4 degrees C or below or using a microwave. No food is to be thawed at room temperature to prevent growth of pathogens and/or their toxins.

Improper Cleaning, Sanitizing of Equipment and Utensils

IN COMPLIANCE - Observed sanitizing solution to be available for use at time of inspection. However, it was measured to be too strong.

Ensure that 100 ppm chlorine sanitizing solutions are prepared by adding 1 oz. of unscented bleach in 1 gallon water. Sanitizing solutions too strong can damage surfaces, hands and leave a residual on food contact surfaces that may then contaminate food.

Improper Cleaning, Sanitizing of Equipment and Utensils

OUTSTANDING - Observed meat slicer to have an accumulation of food debris on the blade. Operator stated that the meat slicer is not used often.

Ensure that the meat slicer is taken apart and all pieces are cleaned (using soap and water) then sanitized using 100 ppm chlorine solution (1 oz. unscented bleach in 1 gallon water) after use to prevent the growth of pathogens on the equipment and the subsequent contamination of food that is prepared using the meat slicer.
