Lack of/Inadequate Food Safety Plan

Temperature monitoring of the coolers must occur, at a minimum of once a day. Temperature logs were provided for this purpose.

 1 0

Inadequate Cooling and/or Refrigerated Storage of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

The pizza preparation cooler was tested and recorded at 4 C and below. Ensure the lids are in place and covering all foods, during slower times of service. As well, the food products were lowered in the inserts and replenished more often from below.

 0 1

Inadequate Cooling and/or Refrigerated Storage of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

The front preparation cooler was tested again and reached 4-5 C. Continue using the following tips: 1. Do not stack inserts. 2. Use metal inserts for potentially hazardous foods. 3. Use probe thermometers for monitoring food temperatures. 4. Keep large lid closed as much as possible, and cover potentially hazardous foods with individual lids. 5. Service the cooler so that the bottom portion is storing food as close to 0C without freezing. 6. Do not overfill inserts.

 0 1

Improper Cleaning, Sanitizing of Equipment and Utensils

Information was provided to the bartender, Duncan and the sous chef, Alex, the glasswasher and dishwasher are not permitted to operate with quaternary ammonium. These machines must use iodine or chlorine. The glasswasher was checked and iodine is used for this unit. Ensure a sanitizer log is maintained daily. Iodine kit was available and the concentration must equate a minimum of 12.5 ppm to 25 ppm. The low temperature dishwasher must be a minimum of 50 ppm of chlorine. Ensure the chemicals are changed to effectively sanitize dishes.

Handsink was accessible at the time of the inspection.

Inadequate Insect/Rodent Control

Back door was closed, during the inspection, however if it is to be prompted open, a screen door must be installed. This door will refrain rodent or pest entry.
