In Compliance - Food

Food handling practices appear to be satisfactory.

Refrigeration temperatures are satisfactory (< 4 degrees C / 40 degrees F).

Food storage practices appear to be satisfactory.

No pests observed at time of inspection.

Maintenance of premises is satisfactory.

Equipment, utensils and food contact surfaces used on the premises are washed and sanitized in a manner that removes contamination.

Improper Sanitary Facilities

Improper Sanitary Facilities

Observed no cold water available at one of the handwashing stations in the women's washroom. The other handwashing station was equipped with both hot and cold running water. Maintenance personnel has been contacted to address the issue.

> Ensure that both handwashing stations are equipped with hot and cold running water.

Improper Construction/Maintenance/Sanitation of Establishment

> Thoroughly clean all hard to reach areas on a routine basis. > Sweep up accumulated debris under the equipment and bar service area. > Clean with soap and warm water then sanitize with 100ppm bleach solution or with 200ppm quaternary ammonium.

WARNING, REPEAT INFRACTION: Observed an accumulation of bottle caps, straws and garbage under the equipment.

Improper Construction/Maintenance/Sanitation of Establishment

> Discard all the ice. Clean with soap and warm water, then sanitize. > Allow sanitizer to air dry > Refill the ice machine.

Observed dirt accumulation on the ice machine lid and on the stopper inside the ice machine.

 1 0

Improper Construction/Maintenance/Sanitation of Establishment

> Thoroughly clean the unit with soap and warm water, then sanitize with 100ppm bleach solution or 200ppm quaternary ammonium. > Ensure that the floor is mopped on a routine basis. > Follow cleaning schedule (Sanitation plan)

Observed dirt and debris accumulation on the stainless steel cooling unit in the bar area. This unit is currently not used for cold storage. Door handles were sticky. The flooring in the bar area was sticky and dirty.

Improper Construction/Maintenance/Sanitation of Establishment

> Tightly chain the carbonated cylinder tanks around the body of the tanks to a secure permanent structure.

Observed unchained carbonated cylinder tanks in the storage room beside the ice machine. In the event of an accident, the tank could tip over and cause injury to staff or customers.
