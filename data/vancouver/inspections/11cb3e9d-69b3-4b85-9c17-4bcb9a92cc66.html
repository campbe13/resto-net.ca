Improper Construction/Maintenance/Sanitation of Establishment

Ensure that work tools are placed in a manner away from food preparation, and cleaning and sanitizing areas to prevent potential contamination. Ensure that contact surfaces are durable, smooth, impervious and easy to clean. Cloth can become moist, harbour pathogens and spread disease. Ensure that the back screen door has the ability to completely shut to prevent pest infestation. A fly issue has been noted by the pest control company. If the back screen door cannot fully close, it may contribute to a fly issue. Ensure that the staff washroom has a self-closing mechanism to prevent potential contaminants from entering the kitchen. Ensure that a door sweeper is properly fitted at the bottom of the back door to prevent the entrance of pests.

RESOLVED: Tools were not found to be stored near food contact surfaces. Dishware storage racks did not contain any cloth to absorb moisture. Back screen door is fixed so that it completely shuts. Staff bathroom door now equipped with an automatic hinge so that it can be automatically shut. NEW: The bottom of the back screen door has a large enough gap that could potentially allow pests to enter.
