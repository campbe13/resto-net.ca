In Compliance - Food

Refrigeration temperatures are satisfactory (< 4 degrees C / 40 degrees F).

Freezer temperatures are satisfactory (< -18 degrees C / 0 degrees F).

Hot holding temperatures are satisfactory (> 60 degrees C / 140 degrees F).

Lack of/Inadequate Food Safety Plan

Temperature logging is in place but not up to date.

Ensure temperatures are recorded at least once daily for every unit holding perishable foods to monitor safe storage of foods.

 1 0

Food Not Protected From Contamination - Microbiological

One container of salad greens was seen being placed into a reach-in cooler beneath raw meats and seafood. Kitchen staff was instructed to relocate this immediately.

Ensure all vegetables and ready to eat foods are stored above raw meats to prevent the risk of cross contamination.

 1 1

Food Not Protected From Contamination - Other Sources

Open canned goods must be transferred into a food grade container upon opening to prevent metal contamination.

One can of tomato paste was seen stored in its orginal container after opening. Instructed one kitchen staff to transfer contents out of the can immediately.

 1 1

Food Not Protected - General

Provide scoops with handles for food to prevent contamination. Ensure foods are dispensed in a sanitary manner.

Food dishes are being used for scooping ingredients.

 1 0

Food Not Protected - General

Foods in walk-in cooler were generally stored uncovered. Some foods in walk-in cooler as well as in the kitchen were stored directly on the floor.

Provide food grade covers for food containers to prevent contamination. Ensure all foods are stored at least 6 inches off the floor to facilitate cleaning and to prevent contamination of foods from splashes.

 2 0

Food Not Protected - General

Ensure potentially hazardous foods are stored at 4 degrees C or colder or 60 degrees C or hotter at all times. Perishable foods stored in the danger zone in excess of 2 hours must be discarded.

Numerous containers containing meat were seen stored at room temperature. Temperature was measured to be 18 degrees C. Kitchen staff stated it has been left out of the fridge for more than 2 hours. The following were discarded at the time of inspection: - 1 container chicken - 2 containers breaded porkchops - 1 container fish fillets - 1 plastic tote fried porkchops - 1 container steak

 1 0

Improper Construction/Maintenance/Sanitation of Establishment

Food debris and grease build-up on shelves in walk-in cooler, dry storage room, and kitchen.

Ensure to thoroughly clean and sanitize shelves on a routine basis to prevent build-up and contamination.

 1 0

Improper Construction/Maintenance/Sanitation of Establishment

Ensure to seal any holes and gaps bigger than 1/4 inch to elimination pest entry points.

2 holes were observed at the base of the east side wall in dry storage room.
