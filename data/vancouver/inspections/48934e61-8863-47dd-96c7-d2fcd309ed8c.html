Inspection Information

Observations # of New Observations # of Resolved Observations

Lack of/Inadequate Food Safety Plan

A written food safety plan must be developed.

 1 0

Inadequate Cooling and/or Refrigerated Storage of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

When using ice to keep foods cold (like the fresh seafood display), be sure to bury the foods in sufficient ice. Monitor the temperature of the sushi display cooler very closely to ensure that a temperature of 4 degrees C is maintained at all times. Food temperatures must be monitored more frequently, especially during or immediately after busy lunch and dinner periods to determine the effectiveness of your refrigeration units and ice displays during periods of peak demand. Ensure that monitoring of the condiment wells also takes place. All results are to be recorded. Time controls must used for the salad bowls in the salad preparation station. Please ensure that bowls that are in use are not stacked on top of one another. Immediate corrective action is required.

 4 1

Inadequate Hot Holding of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

Time controls must be implemented for the high-hazard sauces such as Hollandaise and Bechamel which are being stored at room temperature on the cooking line. A written food safety plan specific to these items must be developed and forwarded to Vancouver Coastal Health for approval. The food safety plan must address how the safety of these high hazard foods will be assured using time controls. Immediate corrective action is required.

 1 0

Improper Cleaning, Sanitizing of Equipment and Utensils

The dishwasher's chorine sanitizer concentration must be tested at least twice a day to verify that dishes are being properly sanitized. Immediate corrective action is required.

 1 0

Inadequate Insect/Rodent Control

Please ensure that rodent control devices are not moved away from the walls or rotated - doing so will render these devices ineffective.

 1 0

Improper Construction/Maintenance/Sanitation of Establishment

Cleaning is required inside the deep fryer cabinets. The ice machines and the ice storage unit in the main bar must be cleaned and sanitized on the inside. All buckets and scoops used to dispense and haul ice must be cleaned and sanitized at the start of each shift. Please remind staff that ice must be protected from contamination at all times. Over-turned dairy crates are not to be used to store foods and equipment. Proper shelving/tables must be provided. The kitchen garbage can requires thorough cleaning. Dust which has accumulated under the pizza oven must be removed. Slight cleaning of the compressor in the walk-in produce cooler is required to remove dust and mildew. The electrical room adjacent to the dishwashing area must be thoroughly cleaned and organized. Immediate corrective action is required.

 8 1

Improper Construction/Maintenance/Sanitation of Establishment

The small dry storage area next to the convection oven is not suitable. All spillage must be removed and all dry goods must be relocated to an area which is well-ventilated, well lit, easy to clean and where the foods will be protected from contamination. Proper shelving is to be provided so that products can be stored at least 6 inches (15 cm) up off of the floor. The dry storage area under the stairs must be thoroughly cleaned and reorganized. All spilled syrup and other debris on the floor must be removed. This area is extremely small and it is imperative that goods be neatly stored at least 6 inches (15 cm) up off of the floor to facilitate cleaning and maintenance. The ceiling tiles directly adjacent to the dry storage area are badly discolored and must be cleaned or replaced. Dust located directly above the convection oven has been removed, but the surface is now no longer smooth, washable and non-absorbent. The surface must be refinished with a smooth,scrubbable, heat resistant material.

 8 4

Improper Construction/Maintenance/Sanitation of Establishment

The missing ceiling tile over the 2 compartment sink must be replaced. Tape and shipping wrap must be removed from all equipment such as the ice machines, the french fry cutter, etc. Damaged wall and ceiling surfaces in the main kitchen corridor leading to the back door must be re-finished so that the surfaces are smooth, washable and non-absorbent. The lighting in front of the pizza oven is still too dim and must be increased to at least 50 foot-candles. Second Warning.

 4 0

TF: NO Trans Fat documentation for food is kept on site or provided on request

Written documentation must be on-site with respect to the percentage of trans fat in your menu items. Second Warning.
