Food Not Protected From Contamination - Other Sources

The following contravention is now resolved: Dry Ingredients Observed an open bag of pastry flour on the shelf at the dry storage area downstairs.

All opened bags of dry foods should be contained in food grade containers with fitted lids to protect from the risk of contamination.

Food Not Protected From Contamination - Other Sources

Clear separation between potentially hazardous foods, raw foods, cooked/not cooked food products should be maintained. Colour coded tongs should be considered and tong cleaning should be in accordance with your sanitation plan.

The following contravention is now resolved: - The chef noted that designated tongs are used on the cooking line. Food Handling Tongs Observed same tongs used for both raw and cooked food items at the main kitchen.

 1 1

Food Not Protected From Contamination - Other Sources

The following contravention is NOT RESOLVED: - Raw salmon was stored in the drawer above bread. This was corrected at the time of inspection. Ready to eat food items must not be stored below raw meats to prevent meat juice contamination. Cooler Drawer In the cooler drawer across from the stoves, raw beef was stored in a drawer above garlic bread.

Raw meats must always be stored at the bottom drawer to prevent meat juice contamination of other food items. Please move raw meat to the bottom drawer.

 1 1

Inadequate Cooling and/or Refrigerated Storage of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

Ensure all potentially hazardous foods are stored out of the danger zone (4 to 60C). When an ice bath is used, inserts must be fully immersed in ice and ice must be changed routinely

The following contravention is NOT RESOLVED: - Ice baths were again low on ice. Ice was added at the time of inspection. Inserts must be immmersed in ice to be adequately cooled. Ice Baths Potentially hazardous foods were stored in ice beside the deep fryer. These were measured at 11C. Other potentially hazardous foods were observed in ice across from stoves at 12C.

These bulbs can shatter and contaminate the prep area under it. Please install light covering tubes to prevent possible shattered glass contamination.
