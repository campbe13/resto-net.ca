Lack of/Inadequate Food Safety Plan

A food safety plan must be on-site at all times. All staff must be familiar with the contents and location of the plan. Second Warning. Please forward a copy of your food safety plan to Vancouver Coastal Health before October 30, 2011.

 1 0

Inadequate Cooling and/or Refrigerated Storage of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

Foods being stored in the main salad bar were too warm: yogurt: 8.3 degrees C cottage cheese: 8.8 degrees C tuna: 9.9 degrees C stewed mushrooms: 10 degrees C salsa: 12.4 degrees C refrigerated cabinet below: 8.2 degrees C These food temperatures are unacceptable. Potentially hazardous foods must be maintained at 4 degrees C or colder at all times. Immediate corrective action is required. Food temperatures must be monitored throughout the day. Results are to be recorded in a logbook. Second Warning.

 1 0

Improper Cleaning, Sanitizing of Equipment and Utensils

There was no sanitizer on the premises. This is unacceptable. A solution containing at least 100 ppm chlorine or 200 ppm quaternary ammonium compound must be on-site at all times. Immediate corrective action is required.

 2 1

Lack of/Inadequate Sanitation Plan

A written sanitation plan must be on-site at all times. Second warning. Please forward a copy of your sanitation plan to Vancouver Coastal Health before October 30, 2011.

 1 0

Improper Sanitary Facilities

Access to the hand wash sink was blocked. This is unacceptable. The hand wash sink must be accessible at all times. Immediate corrective action is required.

 1 0

Improper Construction/Maintenance/Sanitation of Establishment

General sanitation is marginal and must be improved. The broken plexiglass doors on the display cooler must be replaced. All tape must be removed. Cleaning is required under the sinks. Second warning. The shipping material must be removed from the side of the ice machine.

 4 0

Food Handler Training

The food handler present had not completed Foodsafe. This is contrary to the BC Food Premises Regulation. At least one person per shift must be present at all times who has completed Foodsafe or an equivalent food handler training program. Second Warning.

 1 0

Other - Inadequate Ventilation

A second donair machine has been added and the donair machines have been installed in a new location. In addition, pan-frying is taking place. The donair machines and pan-frying activities create grease-laden vapors. Please be advised that operation of the donair machines and all pan fyring activities must cease since there is no means of extracting the grease-laden vapors. Should you wish to operate the donair machines and continue to use the heating plates for pan frying, an approved commercial exhaust extraction system (fumehood) with fire suppression must be installed. Plans for the installation of this fumehood would have to be submitted to the City of Vancouver, as well as Vancouver Coastal Health, for approval in advance of the installation. Please be advised that this matter has been referred to the City of Vancouver Building and Fire Departments

TF: NO Trans Fat documentation for food is kept on site or provided on request

Written documentation must be on-site with respect to the percentage of trans fat in your menu items. Second warning.
