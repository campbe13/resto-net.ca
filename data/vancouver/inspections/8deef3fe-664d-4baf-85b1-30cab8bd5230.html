Food Not Protected - General

Bread THE FOLLOWING VIOLATION IS NOW IN COMPLIANCE - Loaves of bread were stored unprotected. All stored bread should be in food grade containers with fitted lids, no bread must be left unprotected outside business hours.

Inadequate Insect/Rodent Control

Mice THE FOLLOWING VIOLATION IS NOT IN COMPLIANCE - Widespread mice activity was oberved at the facility. All areas of the facility should now be thoroughly cleaned and sanitized, your pest control contractor made aware of the ongoing situation to carry out more concentrated treatment, mice entry holes sealed closed using a hard filler (do not use expanding foam for this task) and existing expanding foam removed and replaced with a permanent filler/cover. THIS IS A REPEAT VIOLATION - mice droppings were again observed in the area of the hot water tank, in the dishwasher area and on the shelves in the kitchen.

 1 0

Improper Construction/Maintenance/Sanitation of Establishment

Water Leak THE FOLLOWING VIOLATION IS NOW IN COMPLIANCE - There is a water leak inside the right hand prep fridge. This should now be repaired leaving in good working order. Food Slicer THE FOLLOWING VIOLATION IS NOT IN COMPLIANCE - The food slicer was in need of cleaning. It should now and routinely, after each period of use, be thoroughly cleaned and sanitized. Make-up Air THE FOLLOWING VIOLATION IS NOT IN COMPLIANCE - The make-up air ventilation outlets and surrounding ceilings were dirty indicating the make-up air filters are in need of replacement. To prevent risk of poor air quality, air filters must be regularly replaced, these should now be replaced. - no evidence of filter change available.

Improper Construction/Maintenance/Sanitation of Establishment

Cleaning THE FOLLOWING VIOLATION IS NOT IN COMPLIANCE - Cleaning was generally very poor. All areas of the facility including but not limited to; floors, walls, shelves, fridges, stove should now and routinely be thoroughly cleaned using a degreaser and sanitized. THIS IS A REPEAT VIOLATION Light Fitting THE FOLLOWING VIOLATION IS NOT IN COMPLIANCE - The light fitting within the rear kitchen was not working. This should now be repaired or replaced leaving in good working order. Exhaust Ventilation System THE FOLLOWING VIOLATION IS NOW IN COMPLIANCE - The exhaust ventilation duct system is now overdue for inspection. For good fire precaution practices this should be cleaned at least once every six months. Food Contact Surface THE FOLLOWING VIOLATION IS NOT IN COMPLIANCE - Some food contact surfaces have exposed areas of wood. All exposed wood should now be sealed leaving a smooth and cleanable surface.
