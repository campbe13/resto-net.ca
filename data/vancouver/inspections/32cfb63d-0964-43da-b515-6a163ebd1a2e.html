Implement an Integrated Pest Management Program which includes: Monitoring, Cleaning, Sanitizing, Pest Proofing, and Abatement Services. Pest Control visits every two weeks. Undersigned read the November report. Spoke to Store Manager who stated the pest control notified of a number of areas that needed sealing. Seal the remaining holes observed during inspection with steel wool and cover with durable construction material to prevent entry or exit of pests. Continue to conduct pest proofing to eliminate problem. Install door sweeps on the two doors to the commissary. wo doors into the commissary causing a gap for rodents to enter.
